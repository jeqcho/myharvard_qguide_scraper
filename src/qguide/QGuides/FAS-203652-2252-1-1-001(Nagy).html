The platonic ideal of senior spring courses?
I took this class my final semester at Harvard and it is by far ,my favorite class that I have taken during my time in college! Take this class!!! I learned a lot about Sappho and Ancient Greece while developing a more robust appreciation for the classics. I really enjoyed this class and think everyone else would too. Also, Professor Nagy is such an intelligent, kind, soul. His teaching style makes you want to show up to class even on a Monday
This class is awesome!! Greg is an amazing professor and is so enthusiastic about his topics
TAKE THIS COURSE! Professor Nagy is truly a light in the Harvard world: he has so much experience in the academic universe of Harvard, and is specifically wise in this discipline of Lyric/Sappho. The class environment was so wonderful – Professor Nagy encourages the agency of the students who self select for this class. I sincerely appreciated his encouragement and flexibility for us students to engage in topics we are passionate about within the framework of the course, and share them with the class! In that way, only take this course if you are a self–motivated student with an idea of an individual framework with which to guide your study – most of your work will be self generated (which is wonderful if that is right for you – as it was for me!). Thank you Greg for the most amazing semester, I will certainly take a course with him again :)
