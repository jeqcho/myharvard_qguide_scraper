Take this class if you want a very overarching view of the mind body connection, integrative healthcare approaches, etc. but not if you are looking for a super in depth scientific understanding of how the mind body connection works. You will get more exposure to the history of mind body connection, how it manifests, case stories, treatment options, etc. than the biological underpinnings of it (it is a psych course not a biology course) so keep that in mind. Very good option for anyone wanting to be any form of healthcare provider one day!
This course has been one of the most transformative classes I’ve taken at Harvard. The teaching, the reading material, and the level of engagement were truly exceptional. Coming from a background in Public Policy, I’ve had the opportunity to experience a wide range of courses here, but this one stands out. It has been instrumental in cementing my desire to become a clinician. In a few years, I plan to apply for a Doctorate in Psychology, a path I would not have considered seriously without this course and Dr. Blanchard’s guidance. If you're interested in supporting people not just intellectually or physically, but through a truly integrated understanding of their biology, psychology, and social environment, this is absolutely the right course for you.Whether you're curious about the mind–body connection, seeking to understand your own lived experience, or even coming from an athletic background, this course offers profound insight and relevance. Take it!
