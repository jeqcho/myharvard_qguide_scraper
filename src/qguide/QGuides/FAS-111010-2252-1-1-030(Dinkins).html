What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This class is really good and super approachable, don't be worried about taking it! Being confused during class is normal since you're first learning and exploring the material, but make sure you understand the psets, as those are generally the same style as the exam questions. Sometimes the pacing in class can be a little fast, but you can go to office hours or mqc to ask any questions you have. If you need extra help or want practice problems, I used khan academy and it clarified a lot of things!
Take this class. It's not easy and it'll push you, but if you have a math brain you'll be fine. However, if you are like me and it takes a long time for certain topics to click, it's challenging because there isn't enough time to nail the concepts between exams. The tests will have you going into it thinking "yeah I know how series work and what they are" and then leaving thinking "nevermind." I think the way they test is very true to the "ability to apply concepts" and I quickly realized I had a hard time applying concepts. Don't be discouraged because there is a lot of support for this class but I would say don't expect an easy A at all.
This course is quite challenging, so you should thoroughly go through the content and material after each class. Make use of the tons of resources that are available to you. The problem sets are also quite challenging; however, if you make use of the resources and pay attention in class and materials, you should be good. The tfs are also very nice and the CAs are also nice.
