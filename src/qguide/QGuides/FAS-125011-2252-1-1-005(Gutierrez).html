Spanish 20 is great! It really helped me grow as a speaker and explore the culture.
Professor Arteta is the absolute best. This class is not a lot of work if you have a basic grasp on Spanish and feel confident speaking up in class and being confident and okay with making mistakes.
This class isn't difficult as long as you do the readings and remember at least the main details/messages. The hw is very fair, the class is fair. The TF can make or break your experience. I had jorge and he was great and always willing to do extra office hours which helped me succeed a lot.
This class's content is laid out very well with a calendar on the home page of the canvas course. Take advantage of it as it will help make scheduling plans for when you plan to complete certain assignments. You should make sure to develop a study habit as that will make learning vocab and conjugation structures easier, but also because the homeworks are automatically graded, so if you do them late you automatically get a penalty. It's only 10% per day, and TFs can give back points at their discretion, but it's just easier to get in a habit of doing them. My TF, Jorge Arteta, is GREAT and really understanding, which is great when you are struggling with a concept since he really tries to help you understand!
Jorge Arteta will teach you a lot about the language of spanish. It may not be the actual content of the class but you will learn spanish by diffusion from him. He is a fair grader, very personable, and flexible. He teaches language the way it should be taught. Best language teacher I have had in my 8 years of language. He is clearly liberal and does opine on politics sometimes.
