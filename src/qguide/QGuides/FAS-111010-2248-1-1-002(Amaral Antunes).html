TAKE THIS CLASS! Even if you are someone who remotely enjoyed math in high school, I would recommend taking this class. This class had some of the best TFs in the math department who all are really making sure you are going to succeed in math and all your classes. They are constantly working o make sure each student is learning the material to the best of their abilities and are truly a great group of teachers! the material is also so much fun, you just have to be curious always in class.
In general, this course covers basic mathematical concepts: integrals, series and differential equations. If you feel like you could take 21a right away, you should probably shop it in the first few weeks.
If you've done Math AI IB HL, then you should still probably take this class. It's non–calc, so you will have to get used to that. Also the content on math AI IB HL is different from the content of this class, especially if calculus wasnt your strongest subject in IB math which was my experience. Like you learn the very basics of why integration works here and everything is done from the ground up. It's just a pretty good math class, not too hard but 3 psets a week is a workload so be ready.
This class is a good way to get an introduction to college math. In my case, I really liked my professor, Jonier, and he made everything easy to understand and clear. However, be warned that this course can take a lot of time in terms of psetting. Many times I felt like the psets stretched and became unreasonably long, in situations in which it wasn't really contributing to my knowledge of mathematics. I took this course to learn about series and differential equations, but I feel like maybe if I had known before what would be covered in this topic, I would've just self–studied it during summer to catch up and gone with 21a instead. That being said, this course is very comprehensive, and it could be a great starting point for many people. Just be sure to check the topics covered to see if this is a good starting point for you!
Take if you like math or need to satisfy a requirement, but be aware of the heavy workload of the psets
