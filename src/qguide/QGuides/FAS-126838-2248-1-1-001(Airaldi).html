TAKE THIS CLASS! Even as an experienced actor this course was such a gift and I am so glad to have taken it. First of all, the professor Remo knows exactly what he is doing and takes all of us as students on such a journey of growth and closeness with each other. Having a class as a freshman with only 12 other students who we are interacting with so vulnerably, is only made possible because of the safe and supportive environment in this class. People in this course ranging from barely no experience acting to those who have acted professionally all grew and Remo was able to tailor his coaching to each person's experience. Seeing the weekly shows is such a gift and a perfect introduction to arts in Boston!
I would tell students to prepare to be challenged and to open yourself up in this course. Prepare to have fun and to see some amazing shows and to become pretty close with the people you work with in this class and with Remo, the professor. This course is so fun and formative and was such a pleasure to be in so I do highly recommend especially if you like acting and performance but don't know exactly if it is for you or if you're good at it. This helps you build so many new skills and a whole new perspective on acting if you have done some but aren't a professional.
Take it!! Its great.
THIS CLASS IS PERFECT FOR YOUR FRESHMAN YEAR!! I recommend with enthusiasm for anyone wanting a chill fun class with a bunch of theater!
PLEASE take this class! Whether you have taken years of acting classes, or this is your first one, I promise that Remo will teach you something new and in an engaging way. Though they are a bit time–consuming and sometimes inconvenient, the plays we went to see every other week were AMAZING. I especially loved Sojourners, it was my favorite play that I've ever seen and I would have never been exposed to it had it not been for Remo and this class.
Become good friends with your classmates in this class because it sure makes it more fun.
This course was buzzing with energy and positivity. The environment was constantly filled with warmth, encouragement, and curiosity. Every student was encouraged to go above and beyond, and make an utter fool of themselves, which proved to be incredibly eye opening.
