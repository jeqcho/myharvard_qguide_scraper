Comments
Take this course if you are interested in Daoism, Confucianism, Buddhism, Shintoism, etc. It can be a bit of reading here and there but the assignments are fair and for the most part, the material is pretty interesting and nuanced.
It is an awesome class run by an awesome professor.
Really really big fan of this class, and would highly recommend to anyone interested in learning about East Asian religions (which I've long heard about but never had a chance to properly study). Professor Robson gives perhaps the most interesting, educational, and compelling lectures I've ever witnessed –– I actually felt motivated to go to each one out of fear that I would be missing (probably goes hand–in–hand with finding the subject matter interesting). Also, Trevor was a fantastic TF and lead sections that really highlighted the most important content to remember. Class assignments are two take–home quizzes and a final paper –– a chill workload, and it was actually kinda enjoyable.
