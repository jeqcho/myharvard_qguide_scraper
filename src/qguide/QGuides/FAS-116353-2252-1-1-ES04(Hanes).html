TAKE EXPOS STUDIO. Especially if you're a pre–med cause you need a year of English anyway. Studio is honestly so much better than standard because there is more time for feedback, you have more time to focus on your papers ––– and it is better writing. For standard, I saw all of my friends worry and rush through everything, while for Studio, even with taking 5 classes, I was rushing but I felt like there was always time to improve papers. I loved my papers and its topic ––– there is a lot of flexibility in choosing topics which I feel like sometimes standard expos might lack.
If you liked studio 10 take studio 20 too!! Especially as an international student with little to no experience.
This class is a little boring at times, but if you are willing to participate, you will get a lot out of conversations with your classmates.
Dr. Hanes is AMAZING! Expos has become my favorite class all semester because of her!!!
