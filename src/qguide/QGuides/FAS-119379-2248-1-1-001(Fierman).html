TAKE THIS COURSE!!! If you have any interest in Anthro, take this class, especially if it's with Dr. Fierman. She is incredible, and the course is amazing. You won't find yourself completing assignments just to do them, but instead, you will inevitably gain a deeper understanding of the topic. The mini–ethnography project is truly so fun and worthwhile. There is a lot of reading, but you will figure out how to manage it, and you will gain so much from this class.
Prof Fierman and Haden are both truly wonderful, down–to–earth human beings. They keep it real, and facilitate great discussions. The readings are all great and give some teachable takeaway, and the class discussions/assignments are very chill.
What you get out of this class is entirely dependent on how much you want to put in. Really fascinating if you’re willing to engage with the material.
I would say Julia was a great instructor and pioneered our learning to fully understand what ethnography is. The writing is a bit difficult to adjust at first but when you get the hang of it it is okay. Don't be scared or nervous to ask for help.
Great class! An amazing learning experience and really helps you find the ethnography that resonates with you
This course was my favorite this semester! Unfortunately, I think individual students' experiences are very much dictated by the course instructors, so my experience might not be applicable to future iterations of this course, but I loved my time in the course. That said, this course was a really helpful introduction to a wide array of ethnographic methods, with some attention to trends in the discipline and priorities for future scholars. While the majority of students in my class had some prior coursework in anthropology, that experience was not necessary to succeed.
Such an amazing course! Professor Fierman and Haden are amazing and push you to think about ethnography and anthropology from so many different perspectives.
Anthro 1610 is an AMAZING course, and Professor Fierman is one of the best. Anthro concentrators should be excited about taking 1610!
