What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Chinese 130b was an excellent continuation of Chinese 130a. The course staff are very understanding and supportive and will be there to help you and listen to your concerns if you're worried about any of the assignments. The Chinese language instructors are always some of the quickest to respond to emails out of any other course I've ever taken at Harvard. The quizlets were extremely helpful for preparing for the in–class quizzes and exams. Pay attention in class and you learn most of what you need to do well on the assignments. It was fun to chat with actual students from China through the Chinese language mentor program and a great low–risk practice opportunity.
