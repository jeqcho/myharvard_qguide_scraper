Everything was amazing. Zach is fantastic.
This class will teach you things about yourself as you learn who you are with your classmates, but more importantly, it will continue to teach you things about yourself. Definitely one of the best classes I've taken over my four years here. Zachary Stuart is an amazing preceptor, and I wouldn't have taken nearly as much away without his engaging energy — ie we would warm up at the beginning of class by dancing, stretching, etc which seems a but odd at first, but immediately forces you out of your comfort zone, making it easier to deliver speeches .... small things like this that he does makes him a rare gift of a teacher!
You get out what you put into this course, but even if you don't put in much, you will still gain confidence in your voice, a greater understanding of your values and what you care about, and 10 new friends.
The biggest benefit of taking this class, which I did not expect, was being able to listen to classmates speak about topics of their choice. You'll make loads of new friends and learn new things. I will say, I didn't get as much constructive feedback as I was expecting... don't expect the class to be life–changing, skill–wise. But, it's a great class to take senior year. Low workload and fun.
Unfortunately Zach will no longer be teaching this, and he really made this class. In general, great experience as everyone else is echoing. I'm sure the other instructors are great too.
