This was a fantastic course that offers an involved but friendly introduction to integrable probability, from a pioneer of the field. You will learn about the connections between several important areas of mathematics, namely probability theory, representation theory, combinatorics, and analysis. You will also be introduced to many interesting problems, many of which are still areas of ongoing research.
