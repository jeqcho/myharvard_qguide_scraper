Take KOREAN BA!! It is so so much fun and you learn so much along the way. The teachers and section leaders are all incredibly nice and understanding, and they really work with you to improve your language skills and to have a good grasp on the assignments. They really don't want to stress you out and prioritize the learning experience instead. They are so sweet and make the course so worth it!! Aside from that, Korean is also just a fascinating language to learn and you really learn so much in such a short amount of time.
Be prepared to learn a ton! There are multiple assessments every week, but there is a lot of support. This is not a light course, even for people with some knowledge of Korean.
I learned so much in 1 semester of Korean. Coming from knowing almost nothing, I'm shocked at how much I now know. All of the professors genuinely want you to succeed and the environments in both class and section are so conducive to learning.
Amazing class with amazing professor (love Kim SSN!!). 2 quizzes per week plus biweekly tests can get a bit frustrating, but overall not bad.
PLEASE TAKE THIS CLASS! It is so well taught and fun and the section leaders are always so fun and supportive. Was just a very good time in general. I learned how to speak korean and impress my korean grandma!
One of the most rewarding parts of a language course, and particularly this course, is how quickly you see results, which is a big motivating factor throughout the semester. You gain a very strong foundation in Korean speaking, reading, listening, and writing. The pace is quite fast, however the every day meetings make it manageable to stay on track, and the environment of the class is very encouraging. The homework is very light––I would say the weekly vocab quizzes are what I spend the most time on during a non–testing week.
