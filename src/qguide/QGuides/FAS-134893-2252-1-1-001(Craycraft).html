Comments
This is a fantastic class if you're interested in learning how to engage with people during fieldwork, how to conduct interviews, take notes and interpret site experiences . The class also functions as a survey course, even if you're already familiar with ethnography and anthropology, it presents a broad review of how folklore fits within a larger academic discourse.
Please take this course! Dr. Craycraft is a wonderful instructor, and she tailors the course content to students' interests.
