Also, lectures themselves can be not very interesting. They tend to feel a little disorganized and confusing, and most of the time, you will just spend a lot of time looking at different graphs. I think that this class has the potential to be great, but it is not quite there yet. That being said though, if you need an easy engineering class, I would still take it.

TAKE THIS CLASS!! It is such a good introduction to environmental science and engineering, and the professors are fantastic. They teach relevant material, and all the PSETs, coding assignments, and projects are very interesting. The final project was to go around Harvard or other places, collect air quality measurements using air quality sensors that you build in a lab, and present your findings. I would recommend this class to anyone considering / concentrating in ESE or other engineering concentrations.
