What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
I found this course very valuable and well taught; it built well on German 10AB and, because our language skills were more advanced, featured more interesting readings/movies, more substantive discussions (especially of German history), and more opportunities to write and read substantial texts. My German skills improved a lot, especially reading/writing.
German 20ab is the natural progression following 10ab. As a person who is not naturally gifted at learning languages, I'd recommend with the preface that the course is a hefty amount of work. However, if you managed 10ab just fine, 20ab will be no different. It takes sustained, ritual engagement.
This course is a lot of work, but worth it and an amazing way to get to feel more comfortable with German. The class is challenging and I would say that spending time on homework is important to come prepared, which means that you're doing something every day of the week to prepare. Christina is a wonderful teacher, she never gave up on making people engage even when they were unsure and kept the activities varied and interesting.
