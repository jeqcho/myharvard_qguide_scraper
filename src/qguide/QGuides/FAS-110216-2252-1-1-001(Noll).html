Dr. Noll is wonderful and so educated, and she does a great job of facilitating discussion and asking the hard questions that really make you reflect on your own biases and thought processes. Some parts of the class, I will say, felt like they were dragging on (2 hour class twice a week discussing the same topic) but overall very beneficial. Class was more geared towards sociological structures that perpetuate gender inequalities rather than hard science psychology of genders.
