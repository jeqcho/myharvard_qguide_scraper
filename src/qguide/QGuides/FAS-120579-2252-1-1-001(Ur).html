What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Do the readings intentionally and extensively. Remain open to new ideas and new areas of interest!
This is a great class for someone hoping to explore archaeology and participate in interesting discussions about the organization of the discipline, the field of landscape archaeology, and the role of archaeology in society. The readings are interesting, though there are many, and the professor is very enthusiastic and fun.
This class will heighten your perception of the world around you and invite you to question the intentionality behind its modification, extending that questioning into the past to explore the capabilities and motivations of past peoples.
Do not be afraid of saying what you think in class!
