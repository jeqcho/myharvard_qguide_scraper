What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
I like how every week, students got to pick research papers to present and facilitate a discussion in class. For students whose field of study does not necessarily align with the class's main topics, this encourages them to go out of their comfort zones and research something to facilitate the discussion. The questions we have to submit before the discussion ensured that people at least briefly skimmed the papers. I like how some people walk away from the discussion either with more doubt about the research or are actually more comfortable with the methods. Some geophysics is needed, but you can also search things up as you read the papers. Rebecca is incredible!! She is an expert in the field and gives us a different perspective when we are researching the methods conducted in the papers.
Rebecca is an excellent professor! She did such a good job facilitating student–led discussions, I feel like I learned so much so effortlessly in this class.
