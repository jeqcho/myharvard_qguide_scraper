Don't be too scared for this class if you have to take it!!! It's a super well–structured class. There's a ton of of resources throughout the semester (practice problems, lecture notes, online practice, psets, practice exams, tons of help hours, etc.). It is a big big big time sink – every exam is like a black hole (it takes everything from you as you approach it and you leave it needing weeks to recover haha) but it's definitely possible to do well, even if you don't remember much of AP chem (I took it sophomore year of high school). There's a weekly pset with a group portion, and exams have a take home portion as well that you work to complete with randomly assigned groups after the exam. It's the same exact exam that you took in class, but you complete it as a group and its worth 20% of your grade. Logan is an amazing lecturer and is super super smart. The CA's are really helpful. It's hard – there's a tonnnnn of information to learn and memorize and apply in all kinds of different ways – but it's not too bad in the end. Labs are fun too.
