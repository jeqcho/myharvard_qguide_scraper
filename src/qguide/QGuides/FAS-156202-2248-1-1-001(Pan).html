What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This is a good class for getting experience in working with an industry partner. I believe the workload and satisfaction with the course heavily depends on who your partner and project is. Since you'll be working with your group the entire semester it's also good to find a group that you like and enjoy working with.
You should know who you are working with before you start, and have a clear idea of what your strengths are. You should be able to clearly communicate how you can be contribute to the team and make sure you execute on those things.
Pick your project wisely –– your success in the course is very dependent on what project you are working on and who your TFs are.
It is a good experience for students interested in pursuing research
Make sure to take initiative early on to get your project rolling. If you are confused about expectations or have any disconnect between project partners and course instructors, address those miscommunications quickly.
I really enjoyed this class because I really enjoyed my project topic and my group. We had a 1–hour weekly meeting and maybe 1–2 hours of work outside of that per week since everything was spaced out nicely. Having a whole semester to work on the project was great because we were able to explore the topic in depth.
