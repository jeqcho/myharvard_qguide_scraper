Take this class!!! The first few weeks are a bit rough because you're focused on the fundamentals of small molecules (Kd, experimental assays, etc), but the class really builds into an amazing survey of key high need areas in drug discovery. The speakers are super cool, and most are willing to chat with you after class or go for a coffee. I wish I had taken this class earlier!
I would say that some lectures were amazing and some were not as engaging
Very interesting course. Seminar–style and very little work.
This class was great and straighforward. The TFs were amazing and the course heads chose great speakers to come to class!
This class has the potential to be very good and some lectures are very interesting and well delivered. However, most lecturers are not effective and the course itself is very poorly run. At the time of writing this, only 1 of 3 problem sets have been graded. We also received only 5 days warning to prepare a final (group) presentation during the last week of class. I would not recommend taking this class unless you have to.
This is a great course, probably the best graduate course I've taken at Harvard! The lectures are very interesting, with really good guest speakers coming in every week. Outside of the lectures there's not much to do for this course, except a few homework assignments throughout the semester. But if you go to class those are pretty straightforward. Would 100% recommend.
