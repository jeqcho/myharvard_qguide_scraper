What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Use your thesis seminar leader as a resource — they are there to help you!
I would highly recommend that students think about finding a different TF before taking this course – Grace Michel is not responsive to email and ultimately provides vague and unfocused feedback with little comments on how to improve your work.
If you have a reasonably sized question that excites you, you should write a thesis! Though it can be (incredibly) painful in the moment, I believe the reward is worth it.
If you write a thesis, know that it is no small undertaking. You really need to want to do it. BUT, it's an incredibly rewarding experience where you learn a lot.
