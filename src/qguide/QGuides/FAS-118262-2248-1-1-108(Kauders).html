What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Make conscious effort in evaluating your own writing and ask for constant feedback from instructors.
This course was great to get me in the mindset of writing analytically versus creatively, which was my goal coming into this course and I feel I have accomplished this goal after taking the class. The professors are amazing and very helpful in guiding you along the way. I have really opened myself up to learning how to consider who my audience is and how to develop effective and strong arguments.
This is a helpful class if you have little writing experience, however if you are in–between expos 10 and expos 20, it would be better to choose expos 20 as you will have the opportunity to write about topics that you are more passionate about as opposed to the predetermined topics of expos 10. This class also involves more work than I anticipated, and as someone who is not very passionate about writing, I would've preferred to do a single semester of expos 20 than 2 semesters of a slightly easier class.
Expos 10 is a great option if you don't feel confident about your writing capabilities. Although, if you are only slightly hesitant about writing, take Expos 20.
