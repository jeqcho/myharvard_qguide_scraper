What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Entirely worth it! I would do it again.
This class is an excellent gateway into learning French. Not just the language, but French culture and life (Specifically in Paris) . I was so pleasantly surprised to learn about French literature, arts and living in tandem with the language. It is an intensive class, but seriously there is no better way to do it. You have excellent support structures and a built–in community! Assignments and grading are fair, although hard to maintain.
Despite you need to work hard to keep up with it, you can enjoy the class and have fun with the materials life you've mastered the content!
Please spend time outside of class to engage with material like listening to french music/ tv etc
I took this course out of necessity for my language requirement, but rather than a nuisance like so many other students describe the language requirement at Harvard, I ended up looking forward to this class each day. Meeting five days a week is very time intensive, but it unintentionally causes you to learn French through constant engagement. Plus, spending time with the same people everyday creates a great learning environment where it feels like by the end of the semester you are taking a class with your friends rather than people you met less than two months ago. I appreciate the approach this class takes at language learning; rather than just drilling language through writing and speaking exercises, you learn through learning so much about French history, art and culture. I went into this course admittedly unexcited, but left feeling like I had taken a language, art history and culture class all in one. I would recommend this course to anyway trying to get their language requirement over or just looking for an intro French course at Harvard. Also huge shoutout to Agnes, this course would not be what it was without her. Out of my previous three years at Harvard, she is far and away the most thoughtful TF I have ever had.
