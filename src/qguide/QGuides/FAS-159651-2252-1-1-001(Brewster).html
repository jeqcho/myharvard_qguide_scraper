MCB99B is just like MCB99A with one less workshop and less deadlines...I recommend setting your own deadlines and check–ins with your PIs in this second semester of thesis writing. I also recommend starting to write at the beginning of February/end of January so that you can have your sections checked as you write by your PI and your direct mentor.
This is a typical thesis course, but it is really helpful in terms of flexibility and support!
