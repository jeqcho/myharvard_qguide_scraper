What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This class has no idea what it wants to be. Obviously, if you're reading this you most likely have to take it. The mandatory classes, however, are lowkey the weakest point of the whole English department. This is Harvard's attempt to be Oxford: small class size, focused readings, workshop environments. Except this approach directly contradicts with the whole liberal arts MO of Harvard, and specifically the English department. The readings were almost useless: the class discussions for the first 6–7 weeks of the course were poorly structured and had little to do with the actual Junior paper. It felt like a lot of busy work. The final paper happens really quickly: it seems like it happens within a month. What is the goal of this class? The apparent goal is the junior paper, so why aren't we working on it for a larger portion of the course?
Start your final paper early (which, I know, everyone says)!!!! But seriously, it will save you so much last–minute stress and torment.
