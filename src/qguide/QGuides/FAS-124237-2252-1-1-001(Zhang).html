Take this class!! The teaching staff is wonderful, and the sections are engaging. I will say that if you’ve never taken a Chinese class before, the course moves pretty quickly.
Like Chinese Ba, this class is so excellent, and you will be shocked just how much you learn even in a semester! When I started this semester, I could hold a relatively short conversation in Chinese (the super basics), but Bb adds a lot of useful grammar that you can use in most daily settings. The course was significantly easier for me than Chinese Ba, to the point that I did not quite pay as good of attention as I should have. But, nonetheless, you will learn so much and be so surprised by just how much your speaking/listening will improve.

Also, two pieces of advice: (1) make sure to go to office hours as much as possible, especially if you hope to speak Chinese in conversation; and (2) find ways to keep your Chinese solid over winter break (or whatever break) prior to taking Bb, because your skills will fall off fast!
