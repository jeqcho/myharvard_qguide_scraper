I could not be more thankful that I took this class. I have been meaning to take a creative writing class at Harvard for a long time, and this is all I wanted it to be. The class is writing–intensive, so be ready to put some work in order to get the most out of it. Laura is an absolutely fantastic instructor, and I consider myself very lucky to have taken a seminar with her: her take on writing is really worth thinking about. The class not only improves your writing, but it also gives you a better idea of what kind of writer you are, and it shows you just how important discipline is while writing.
This course is fantastic! It provided a wonderful opportunity for working on your writing, learning from others, and developing your relationship with the short story. Laura was a fantastic teacher who showed genuine care for her students' as writers and people.
This is a great writing workshop. The best way to start writing with an excelent professor.
This class is SO INCREDIBLE. Please take a creative writing workshop before you graduate. I have become more confident as a writer, and I've also developed a deeper appreciation for storytelling (now, I am thinking about writing more seriously in my future). Laura is also so brilliant, supportive, and kind!!
If you have the chance to take the class, DO IT. I learned a lot about writing and got to write some great pieces. Laura is amazing! Her feedback was always helpful and she made sure everyone felt welcomed in class. The class is a wonderful opportunity for anyone who wishes they would have more time for writing. The assignments are enjoyable and flexible enough for you to explore the themes that interest you.
I strongly, strongly recommend this course. Not only is Professor van den Berg so knowledgeable about the skill sets of writing, she is such a gracious mentor and advocate. I have gained so much confidence in my writing, and this course was definitely a breath of fresh air as an Economics concentrator. What a great way to engage in my creative endeavors.
