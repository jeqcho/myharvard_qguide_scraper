This course is an amazing introduction to the English department! 18th century readings specifically centered around the history of the novel itself was not something that I had initially thought that I would be interested in, however, this class was extremely transformative. I was introduced to a new genre of reading, and the structure of the course allowed for a continuous dialogue between the class and professor. Lastly, the teaching staff and course head are both extremely supportive and will help you every step of the way––so do not be intimidated by the reading list!
Take this course if you love reading and novels!
