Great Gov 97 tutorial to take if you're interested in climate policy!
This is a great class if you're really interested in climate change and, more importantly, the policymaking process as a whole. You'll learn a lot about the steps of the policymaking journey, as well as the different stakeholders involved. If you're not interested in public policy and are more interested in political science phenomena that are outside of the policy process.
Pick your Gov97 based on what you are interested in, or else you will become very bored!!
