Take this course if you want to learn about negotiating at a deeper level. Very interesting and engaging content as well as a very caring professor that genuinely wants the best for you.
I really enjoyed my experience in this course– I particularly loved getting the chance to think deeply and critically about various cases we studied including the Northern Ireland Troubles case and the Santa Clara Pueblo people land case. This was a great course for allowing you to consider multiple perspectives on an issue, and consider external factors that may impact the success of a negotiation or the goals of different groups. This course changed the way in which I view negotiations in multiple contexts, and has taught me the importance and value in "moving North–East" to reach an agreement that can benefit both groups. I would say that it is important that you make a schedule for yourself, and start your essays far in advance to ensure you can put together strong drafts. It is also really useful to book appointments with the Writing Center as they can go through your essay with you and give you specific advice.
