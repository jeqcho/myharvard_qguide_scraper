Take the class. It was amazing, and the low number of students was key to bringing us closer together.
Take this class! I am a Computer Science concentrator, and I spoke some German beforehand through cultural interest and a long streak on Duolingo, lol. I kept putting off taking German, because I thought the intensive classes would be too much because of the daily attendance and the pace of the regular ones was too slow. So glad I ended up taking German 10ab! The people in my section were friendly, and we had some lively discussions about all sorts of things. Our professor, Sina, is the kindest and most supportive instructor I met since coming to Harvard. She put in effort to make sure we understood all the material well, but there was still room left for self–learning, which I value a lot. I will definitely be taking more German courses at Harvard!
This class is very rewarding, but note that it will be very time consuming if you want to do well in the class. It moves at a fast pace, but it can be handled once you get used to it. The instructors provide great feedback and are here to work with you!
If you are interested in learning German, take this class! It definitely does take time every day, so I wouldn't recommend it if your other classes are already overwhelming, but I genuinely feel like I have a solid foundation in German after this class, and it's a very fair course.
