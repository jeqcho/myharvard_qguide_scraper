Definitely a class I looked forward to going to.

Her lectures are engaging, thought provoking, intelligent and funny. She will make you see literature in a way you never have before, and make you read some amazing books while you're at it. TAKE THIS COURSE. Even if this exact course is not available, take any course taught by Professor Tara Menon and you will be one of the luckiest college students at Harvard. I was a senior in this class and only wish I had taken a class with her sooner. I had never taken an English class in school and did not expect to, but I would take this class 10x again just to learn from Professor Menon. She is a remarkable lecturer – her enthusiasm for the material is contagious and her intelligence is unparalleled. If you spend one day in Professor Menon's classroom, you will be immediately hooked. She cares so deeply about her students and makes each student feel valued and seen. I could listen to her lecture every day of college.

The length of the books is not a reason to avoid the class – reading the books became a favorite part of my week as each is so engaging, particularly Pride and Prejudice and Middlemarch (take it from someone who does not read for pleasure – I now do, these books are phenomenal). Each assignment is well walked–through and the teaching staff is always there to support you and answer questions. Grading is generous and very fair. Take this class !! Can't overemphasize it enough.
Great class! The reading can be a bit much with Little Dorrit and Middlemarch so just keep that in mind. Professor Menon is a wonderful lecturer and makes very interesting arguments about the novels. This is a new way of lecturing to me but I found it very compelling and I enjoyed the inclusion of data and context in these arguments. I felt like I learned a lot and got to read some really great books in the canon of English literature. The assignments are not typical papers (except the final) and I appreciated the opportunity to engage with the texts in a new way. As a non–English concentrator I was a bit hesitant about the reading for the course, and I wasn't always on track with it, but I'm glad I took to the course anyway. Professor Menon is a bit strict with lateness and attendance, but I respect her rules for her classroom and appreciate the frankness with which she explained them to us. The no phone/electronics policy was also a bit strict but it was nice to write in a paper notebook again (chronic iPad notetaker) and we usually got a wuick break halfway through where we could check our phones anyway so I didn't mind it. Overall, great class and would take again!
