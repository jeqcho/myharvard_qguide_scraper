TAKE THIS COURSE!! Your abilities and knowledge as a student and researcher will grow a lot during this course, and Professor LeBeau is simply amazing. The course’s pedagogical underpinnings helps ensure that all of the assignments are done for a purpose and your learning builds on each other. Even if you don’t have prior experience, you can definitely still learn and succeed. And if not this course, definitely take one of Professor LeBeau’s other courses during your college career!
Take this course!! Kristie is an amazing professor and makes every class super fun and enjoyable –– if you are planning on writing a thesis this class is extremely helpful as well to frame your mind on creating a strong project.
Great class!! Kristie is the best – she really cares about her students and is a great instructor. Coming into this class, I had a basic understanding of the methods used in sociological research, and this class helped me gain a much more concrete understanding of the different methodologies. It also teaches you how to write a strong research proposal, which is a valuable skill – especially if you are interested in academia.
This course was so eye–opening! Prior to enrolling in this course, I had no idea what mixed methods research was but this course gave me such a great overview of the importance of mixed methods research, what goes into creating a robust mixed methods research proposal, and general criticism in the field. This course is super helpful for creating a thesis proposal as well because we really get into the weeds of designing a robust proposal. I highly recommend this course because we also delve deep into coding exercises for qualitative interviews and it's also helpful to hear from lots of experts in the field about the importance of mixed methods research.
