If you are interested in art and the science behind it, take this class!! Professor Samuel knows so much about the topic and is so enthusiastic about it. You get a chance to go on so many cool field trips, meet with art conservators, artists, and so many cool and interesting people. It is a great combination of hands on approach, testing out how the science of optic can help you make better and more realistic art and professor's presentations about the topic. Genuinely such an interesting class!!
This is far and away the best first–year seminar out there for anyone even remotely interested in the topics (and if you aren't right now, you will probably become interested through this course!) We got to do fun experiments, make art that we got to take home, go on field trips to museums and art studios (all during class time so they will not interfere with your schedule), and we got to know Professor Samuel who is such a cool and nice person!!
This course is a wonderful break from regular classes. Make sure to stay on top of the short weekly written responses however as those can start accumulating if you're not careful. I really enjoyed the variety of topics and modes of learning we had, from trying to draw with optical tools, visiting art museums, and directly learning from artists in their own studios.

there are weekly writing assignments, but they aren't too bad. also they are kinda fun sometimes.
This class is fun, engaging, and relaxed, so you can learn for the sake of learning.
