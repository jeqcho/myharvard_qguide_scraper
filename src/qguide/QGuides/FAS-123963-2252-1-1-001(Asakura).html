これまで日本語の学習を勉強してきた皆さん、140Bの授業は強くおすすめします！正直に言えば冬休みに楽をしてしまい、140Bの授業を受けずに卒業しようかと考えましたが。もし140Bの授業を取らなければ、絶対に後悔します。140Bの授業を通して、日本語のあらゆる側面、特に会話力がもっと自信を持って、上手になりました。自分の専攻分野と関係ない話題、例えば安楽死、サブカルチャーや教科書の電子化、いろいろなトピックを触れることができて、本当に役に立ちました！朝倉、日暮両先生は最後まで応援したり、励ましたりしてくださり感動しております。日本語のミス、細かい所まで直してくださり、感謝しております。まとめると、できれば是非140Bの授業を取ってください。
Japanese 140b with Asakura先生 and Higurashi先生 was an absolute blast! I had so much fun in this class because (1) the homework assignments are pretty straightforward and fast, (2) the content is genuinely interesting (rakugo, traditional Japanese stories, subculture, dialects, short stories), (3) I had SOOOO many friends in the class (since I continued from 130ab), and (4) the outside–class events were insanely fun (making mochi, presenting final reports, end–of–year party). Both the teachers are great at engaging the class and sparking interesting discussions. Even tho I took this class mostly 9 am, I never felt sleepy or bored, and I always got a chance to speak. For me, I feel like Japanese language classes have been a consistent highlight of my time at harvard.
Taking Japanese was the best choice I made at this school. The Japanese Language Program teaches Japanese very effectively, and the teachers are flexible and understanding. Through my two years in the program I went from barely being able to speak Japanese to speaking proficiently, and my other skills (reading, writing, and listening) also improved significantly. The class is small, so make sure you get to know your classmates!
