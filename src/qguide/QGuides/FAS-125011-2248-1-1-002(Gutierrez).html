I think the content of this class is really invaluable. You will learn things that were definitely not covered in high school, and it prepares you for a higher level of understanding of Hispanic languages and cultures like no other. However, I do think the instructor makes or breaks the class. I had Ruy and he was amazing.
The more you participate in class, the more you will get out of it. Ruy is very engaging and wants you to make mistakes, and that helped me become more comfortable and confident speaking Spanish. I learned more about the culture and history which was interesting, and the homework never took too much time and was a really good resource to study for the exams. Just be as engaged as possible during class because it helps on the exams.
I really loved this class –– I think it does depend on the professor you get. This class helped me to feel more comfortable speaking in Spanish and also improved my grammar/vocab.
Spanish20 is amazing! It is a perfect step up from Spanish11. My TF Ruy was amazing and really focused on making sure we did not stress over small mistakes. I feel like I really improved my Spanish–speaking skills and learned a lot about culture.
It's super easy – great to fulfill language requirement, but won't teach you much spanish. It's fixated on having serious conversations about things like identity and immigration and discrimination, but I felt like I couldn't practice my spanish that effectively because I was being asked questions that I couldn't even answer in english.
This class is great for building on the foundations of Spanish! It is important to have some background knowledge, but all of the tenses are covered in class which is very helpful.
