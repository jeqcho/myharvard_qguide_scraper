What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
If you have taken Calc AB before and are considering this course, just take 1A. It will save you so much time and is basically the same material just learned at a faster pace.
Math MA is probably the most well paced math course I've ever taken. You never feel like you're falling behind in understanding the material, and if you don't understand something you've got plenty of resources (MQC, Office Hours, tutoring) to help. The 3 PSETs a week seems like a lot (and during midterms it kinda feels that way), but you can usually knock each out in ~2 hours or less. It's an entry level math course, so it's not super challenging. I heard some students complain about how easy it was. If you're on the
fence about MA or 1A, take MA if you want to secure a good grade and don't mind spending an extra semester on math, otherwise I'd say tough it out in 1A.
I wouldn't recommend taking this course if you're also taking two other pset classes (I think with one other pset class the workload would be fine). Overall the course was well taught, but you move through the material really quickly. There's plenty of support, but I would arrange your schedule around office hours to get though the pset in the least amount of time (average 2 hours each).
