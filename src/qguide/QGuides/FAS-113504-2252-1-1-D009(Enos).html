What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Gov 97 is an unfortunate requirement of the Gov Department. If you have to take it, I highly recommend Economic Statecraft as it is one of the few applicable course topics. It was quite interesting to learn about true tools of economic policy and watch in real life as events occurred that we could tie to course topics.
Taegyun was AMAZING! He is a little picky so just make sure you understand the expectations of the assignment. If you follow the rubric and put some time in you will do well. Be present in class and engage. This class is really what you make of it. Economic Statecraft was such an interesting topic and was structured great!
A course on trade policy is hard to come by at the College, so I jumped at the opportunity to take Taegyun's GOV 97! He's a great teacher, and the subject material was extremely relevant in today's world. As part of the broader GOV 97 curriculum, we had 3 writing assignments leading into a final prospectus. Would highly recommend, especially if you're interested in trade and economic policy!
This is a good class to take if you are a government major (also required haha).
