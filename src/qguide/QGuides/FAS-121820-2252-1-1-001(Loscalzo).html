What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This class challenges you, and although all of it may not seem applicable to you and your research, you will get something out of it. You will learn about the interactions of basic science and translational medicine. You will gain a small community of peers that also care about how science impacts health. You will learn how to craft an excellent presentation.
Get started on your presentation weeks before you think you need to!!
I would tell them to start the final project earlier than they think they need and also that if they aren't interested in a high level overview of metabolism with deep dives into the mechanistic aspects of it then they probably shouldn't be enrolled.
I would only take this class if I had a decent biochemistry background and some knowledge of metabolism.
I would highly recommend this course for anyone interested in metabolism. This course provides an excellent overview of human metabolism and dives deep into special topics in metabolism. It is highly interactive and features many guest lecturers.
