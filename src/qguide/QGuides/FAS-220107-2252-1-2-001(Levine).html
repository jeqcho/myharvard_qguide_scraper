I felt a lot of freedom to explore theater and drama, and felt it was a really safe place to try new things! I can't recommend this course enough. It pushed me out of my comfort zone, and I learned so much. David and Elinor are both VERY knowledgeable and were able to tailor this course to be right for the group of students taking it. I LOVED IT!
This course is wonderful if you're into horror, theater, experimental theater, visual fx, etc. It's very experimental, but the topics and direction of the course are based off of student interests. You have a lot of creative power over the work you generate in the course, and you get to learn a lot of basic skills that are helpful for horror–theater. The syllabus also has you watch a lot of classic horror films, which were really great to discuss in class.
