I think all biologists should take a course like this. Many times, you'll see a Nature paper published about some particular mechanism that could easily have been deduced from first principles in systems biology and physics as you will explore in this class. Professor Cluzel is also brilliant and this is a very low–stress class but you still learn a lot. Honestly, the content was super cool and it was really cool to see the overlap of physics and biology.
Great class––especially for AM Bio! Topics are insanely cool, and Professor Cluzel is great!
Amazing course and would absolutely recommend especially if you like math or are interested in biophysics! If you took and enjoyed LS50, it's very LS50esque in content but you definitely learn a lot more. Was one of the highlights of my semester!
***prof cluzel is so kind and knowledgeable! the content itself is very interesting and insightful (kind of ls50–core), but the assignments are very straightforward and approachable. class was part lecture part doing out derivations on the whiteboards which i found to be really fun. cluzel has a ton of interesting anecdotes and perspectives and is just overall a great guy to talk to and get to know.
