What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Such a good class for heritage speakers! It's very interactive and can be quite fun. Could not recommend it any more. The teaching staff is so invested and their teaching style is very effective in making a better speaker and giving you a solid base in writing. If you're wondering whether or not you should take the course, I would recommend if you have no background in reading/writing to be very solid in your speaking ability, and if you're speaking ability is weak, have a background in reading/writing. If you're weak in both areas, this class will be quite a challenge.
Such a wonderful class, would highly recommend for anyone interested in learning or improving their Mandarin! I was initially hesitant since my background in speaking/listening wasn't as extensive as most students in the class, but the instructors are super welcoming and helpful. I thought making it to Vanserg at 9am would be difficult (which it was at times), but this class made it so worth it!
This is an excellent course and the course heads are fabulous and super enthusiastic with teaching Chinese. I highly recommend taking this course! Super supportive team.
Your Chinese will improve dramatically! Don't worry so much about the grade; focus more on learning the language ... after all, learning a new language might be one of the most useful skill you'll pick up in school!
Great class that fosters a solid sense of community. 2 quizzes and a hw per week, which is a lot, but it's a reasonable amount of content to learn. Classes are a lot of fun. Classes are entirely in (basic) Mandarin.
If you have extremely basic speaking and understanding skills, I HIGHLY recommend taking this course as opposed to Ba/Bb. The instructors and incredible, the love to teach and will teach you in an engaging and efficient way. I was surprised by the amount of Chinese I learned and anyone with little to no reading or writing skills should definitely choose this course. However, If you already have these skills, a more difficult class may be a better option for you.
