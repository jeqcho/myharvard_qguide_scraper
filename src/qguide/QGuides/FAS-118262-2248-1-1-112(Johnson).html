What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Manage your time well in each unit, and use the drafting stage for each paper to your advantage.
Jodi Johnson is a truly amazing teacher. His feedback is incredibly thorough, detailed, and clear. So if you want targeted feedback to improve your writing I would recommend Jodi as a teacher. Jodi also makes himself as available as possible whether that be in person or over email. In addition, the classes are very comfortable environments because of the icebreakers at the start of class so people are more open to discussing the papers.

If you are on the fence about Expos 10 versus Expos 20, I would take into account how many courses you want to spend writing and how smoothly you want to transition to university. If you have written many papers in the past couple of years, I would recommend Expos 10 because it is a VERY smooth and slow transition into college writing. It also means that it does not take as much time for your first term so you can have more time to focus on other opportunities.
If you're a pre–med, take Expos 10. I cannot say how much I thought taking this class helped me. While my peers were stressed about their STEM classes and Expos 20 classes being a lot of work, I had a lot of work too but it was quite manageable and the class was really interesting. I was re commended for Expos 20, but taking this was the best choice. Especially if you're a premed, you have to take a year of English so taking Expos series helps. Dr. Jodi Johnson is an amazing and humorous lecturer. It is super fun to be in his class and he gives the most feedback ever. Super helpful and very very accessible to get ––– I would get response to emails within minutes even at midnight. He is also super helpful and holds several Office Hours.
Jodi Johnson is amazing!!!!! Take his section if at all possible. He's extremely understanding and thoughtful, and provides great feedback for unit papers.
The 1:1 experience and learning environment is fantastic and the class is very good.
Absolutely take this class with Jodi. His feedback is great, and he's incredibly funny! I definitely feel more confident as a writer at the end of this course than I did at the beginning.
Take this class! This class was very helpful in transitioning from high school–level writing to college–level writing. In my high school, we focused mainly on timed/ in–class essays. However, this course teaches you how to analyze, compare, and create arguments based on various sources. Do not worry about what others may say about Expos–10, this college experience is your own. Writing is going to be crucial in the next four years at the university, and even in the years beyond that. This class can help build a solid foundation for your writing.
