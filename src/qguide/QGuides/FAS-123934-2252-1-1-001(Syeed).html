Musa is the best and so is this class. Love you guys!!!
This course was great; however, it would have been even better if it had included more students with a genuine and serious interest in film. Screenwriting has a distinct and highly specific form, and choosing to write a screenplay, rather than a novel or short story, should be a deliberate and intentional decision. For many students, it felt like that choice wasn’t grounded in a true engagement with the medium, but rather in a desire to take a light or "fun" creative writing course. Without a strong interest in film, important conversations around cinematic form, structure, and visual storytelling tend to fall by the wayside. As a result, much of the feedback focused primarily on plot, which made the experience feel less like an exploration of screenwriting as an art form and more like a general storytelling workshop. This limited the course's potential to offer a truly transformational creative experience rooted in the unique language of screenwriting. If you're looking for a fun and creative writing course, this one absolutely fits that description. But if you're seriously interested in becoming a filmmaker or screenwriter, particularly for feature films, it may fall short of expectations. Personally, I found that much of the peer feedback I received was fairly one–note, and I came away wishing I had gotten more out of the experience. However, the teacher is amazing and you can get a lot out of him with those things if you engage with him outside of class.
Musa manages to facilitate a great classroom environment which leads to your classmates becoming a family–like community. Screenwriting is a wonderful artistic medium and Musa is great with delivering feedback and creating a space where everyone's art and craft can grow. This is the best class I took at Harvard... Anyone interested in screenwriting take this class.
Musa curated a wonderfully diverse and talented group for our Advanced Screenwriting class. As a senior, I can honestly say this was my favorite class at Harvard. I looked forward to it every week. Musa creates a warm and collaborative environment where feedback and community thrive. He even took us to a film festival screening! He also made time outside of class to give thoughtful and individualized feedback on our work. Musa is not only an incredible teacher but also a genuinely kind person. Take any class you can with him. I am confident the friendships I formed with the 11 other students in this class will last as we continue working in the industry. I am so grateful I took this course.
It is a terrific experience that will greatly hone your abilities as it relates to screenwriting. In particular, this course has provided me with the critical feedback necessary to improve my ability to write dialogue. In addition, it has taught me how to develop a fleshed–out plot from an interesting initial premise.
Highly recommend this course. If you're interested in screenwriting, definitely take it. Musa is awesome and really helpful, and the class is a lot of fun.
