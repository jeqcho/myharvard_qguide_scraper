Presenting on my Senior Thesis held me accountable in ensuring that I was actually progressing. The open forum format always functioned as a source of encouragement and motivation. The feedback of my peers is always valuable! And Eric is extremely skilled in guiding us in the right direction (i.e. possible material to read, etc.).
Eric is phenomenal. He is one of the smartest professors I have ever engaged with. If you take this course seriously, you can really improve your thesis topic, but also learn from others in the class. Go to office hours, even if you are very lost, because Eric will make sure that you leave with a concrete plan.
