What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Take this class to gain a valuable lens into Chinese history through tracing the developments of rural culture and society! All of the assignments were so fun to complete (basically three short creative essays) and game simulations were also conducted during class to immerse students in the learning. Overall, this was a very informative and entertaining class – one that I looked forward to every week!
Take this class! This class has everything you could want: great content where you will actually learn a lot, fun assignments that are challenging in a good way, fair and clear grading, amazing lectures, and interesting discussions in class and in section. This is really a class that does everything right, so I highly highly recommend.
I loved this class! Professor Szonyi is absolutely amazing and incredibly knowledgeable. The course is also quite fun, there are simulation weeks where you are given a character within a particular historical setting. Would totally recommend for anyone who is interested in urban–rural inequalities and modern China in general.
A great class to take even if you have no background in China studies or history! Gives a very important and distinct perspective from rural people whose lives are often overlooked and discounted from the main historical narratives that feed into our current and future views on a society. If this is your first class about China, don’t let it be a reason to not take this! You still get so much out of it and it may require you to do some supplemental research on your own to understand the context and background on some topics and events but talking to the professor or TF for these questions is also a great resource.
I highly recommend this course! It was very eye–opening for me as someone who's studying EAS, and having Szonyi and Will as your teaching duo is truly unbeatable. They made each lecture and section very fun, and the assignments were so low–pressure but so much fun to do and had very high reward. It was such a fun semester with them!
Would highly recommend this class! Teaching team was fantastic this semester.
