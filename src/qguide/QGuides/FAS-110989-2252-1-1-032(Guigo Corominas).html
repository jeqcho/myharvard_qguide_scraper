DO NOT, UNDER ANY CIRCUMSTANCES, TAKE 21B unless you
1. Hate yourself
2. Want to waste your life away in MQC
3. Absolutely have to take this class as a concentration requirement and cannot replace it with any equivalents.

This class was my least favorite that I have taken at Harvard and there are genuinely no words to describe what it did to whatever peace and joy I tried to have this semester. This class taught me that there are two very different types of math people: if you took 21a and liked it, this class will destroy you. If you hated 21a, you might stand a chance. Most of the people I knew who enjoyed the class and did well had seen linear algebra in their (private) high schools which was really disheartening as someone who was seeing everything for the first time. I spend around 15–18 hours on the 3 PSETs per week and I was genuinely considering dropping the class with how overwhelming it was. I am NOT the type of person to dislike a class just because it is hard or new to me, but 21B was difficult in a way that felt unproductive to the process of learning course content because the PSETs were so long and confusing that all I had time and energy for was just trying to finish them in time instead of actually learning. Quiz sessions are helpful but only if there’s a few that fit into your schedule (which was not my situation). The pace of the content did not suit the difficulty of each concept and many parts of the course felt rushed while others felt like tangents from the major topics of linear algebra. Exams are fair but only if you can memorize an insane amount of content and can get good at understanding the annoying multiple choice questions that focus more on minute definitions than a genuine understanding of course content. My biggest regret is not at least trying 22a before committing to the 21 series. 22 is horribly organized and taught from what I have heard, but at least I could have slept at night knowing I didn’t always have a 5 question PSET due every other day that I didn’t understand, and I would have had the benefit of getting a proofs background. TLDR: DO NOT TAKE 21B IF YOU HAVE OTHER HARD CLASSES IN A SEMESTER AND/OR VALUE YOUR HAPPINESS.

The actual 'teaching' that this course provides is the unnecessarily complicated PSets that Chat will walk you through.
