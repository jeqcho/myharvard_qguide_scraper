The course seems like it has very minimal work and Erin repeatedly tells you that it's not difficult to do well in the course as long as you stay on top of content, but the neuroanatomy content (for us, this was Unit 2) was extremely dense and quite challenging! I don't think the class average was particularly great on that exam. Make sure you're on top of the worksheets and TAKE ADVANTAGE OF THE cheat sheet! I don't know why you ever would (some people in the class did) but for God's sake, don't think you can easily get away with 300 slides of content by pure memory & NOT use your cheat sheet for anatomy content.

Other than that, you'll be fine :)
Very interesting class! Dr.Hecht presents very interesting research and material and the work load is very doable. Would definitely recommend being well versed with the material before the exams and creating good cheat sheets.
I really enjoyed this course! The teaching team is passionate about helping students understand content, and the lectures themselves are enjoyable. Assignments are very fair, so if you put in the work you will do well in this course.
A very good class. Dr. Hecht explains the concepts very well and is organized in the flow of the class. Worksheets prepare you for exams very well and line up well with lecture material. Some concepts felt repetitive as an HEB concentrator but a lot were new and interesting to learn about. Teaching staff wants you to succeed and for it to be enjoyable.
TAKE THIS CLASS ITS SO AWESOME!!! If you are at all interested in the brain or evolution this course is great, you really don't need any background so don't worry if you don't have any. The material is so interesting and the class structre is great
this course is a really good one! very manageable with little workload. definitely do the worksheets after each class and do not wait to do them right before the midterm. midterms are easy if you make a good cheat sheet and are familiar with the info. final project was fun and didn't feel very stressful.
Really great class with really great teaching team! Professor Hecht is brilliant and loves the subject which makes her lectures super interesting and passionate!
take this class please. as someone who had no interest in evolution or anything biology related, this clas was so fun and interesting. i would recommend to anyone.
This course had excellent material and a great teaching staff. The topics were interesting, and the lectures were great. I loved my experience in this course. I take away a new appreciation for neurology and a deeper understanding of how brains and brain regions work and have evolved. This course made me realize that there is a lot more to biology than my concentration and what I had learned in previous classes. I feel more well–rounded after this class and I am so glad that I took it. I recommend doing the readings because they are fun, and please pay attention in class. Erin has wonderful things to say, and this really is a great class. It is a lot easier to do well if you pay attention. Because of the note sheet we get on exams, it is easy to think you don't have to learn, but by taking in the information as you go, tests are easier, and this helps build a stronger backbone for the final project components. I recommend this course very highly, and though my concentration in biology is not HEB, I truly loved learning everything in this course. If you enjoy learning, and are interested in an almost intro to neurology course, this is definitely an excellent course for you. I had so much fun and I wish I could have taken all of Erin's classes (as a senior, I'm leaving). Truly truly a magnificent class that was really fun as well!
If you like animals, take this course
