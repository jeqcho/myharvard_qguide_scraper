TAKE THIS CLASS!! Prof. Bell will likely be the most kind and thoughtful professor you have at Harvard!
Having taken many cw workshops, Josh’s is one of the best so far. He’s given me the best feedback I’ve ever received, which hasn’t always been the case. It’s a pretty chill course too which is nice.
Really participate in workshop! It helps build your skills of developing a critical eye for poetry, and lets you figure out what you like in particular about certain pieces. Ultimately, this course makes you a better writer in the sense that you become a better poet, reader, and editor. Josh leaves some of the most thoughtful feedback ever on each piece you write every week. Definitely take this course!!
It’s a great intermediate level course, with lots of opportunities to both work strictly within the bounds of assignments and to do whatever you like!
Take this class!
TAKE THIS CLASS if you like to write poetry! If you're looking for an opportunity to develop your style and get feedback on poems EVERY SINGLE WEEK, take this class! Getting to read peer poems, getting peer and professor review and crit was so amazing and so beneficial to my writing. By the end of the course, I could tell whether the poem I was bringing to the table was strong or not, I could tell as I was writing what feelings my imagery would inflict, whether something was too hard to understand or too on the nose. One of my friends told me that she could see the affect of the workshop on my work before, during, and after I was taking it – it is tangibly stronger than it was before!! So much progress in one semester. Take this.
This class was good, but sometimes, it felt a little draining. I think the class would be better if it was shorter and not packed into a nearly three–hour block. It definitely improved my writing, though!
Couldn't recommend this poetry workshop enough. Josh is so kind, he is generous and caring with his feedback and genuinely cares about all of us. He made the workshop environment feel very supportive and warm, and I think that everyone really got to know each other. I got a lot out of this class and the workload felt balanced too.
