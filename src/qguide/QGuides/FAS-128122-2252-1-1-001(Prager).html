Laura’s class is one of the most perfect examples of a first year seminar, and Laura is one of the kindest professors you’ll meet at Harvard. She genuinely cares for our small group of students, and she brings in food—especially fruit, which we craved—every seminar. Her presentations are engaging and relevant to our own childhoods, and classes are varied with interactive elements like watching videos, reading picture books, and talking to a baby over Zoom. Her three short paper assignments are very fair with fun prompts, and I found them enjoyable to write. If you’re looking for a fun and easy first–year seminar experience, this class is for you.
