***This is one of the best classes taught at Harvard. The material is very interesting and diverse. Outside the classroom, the assignments are quick and different than standard assignments at Harvard. The application of this material ranges far outside of theater lighting. Includes a field trip!
Take this class! Pretty manageable. Not a lot of work load but the assignments do pile up close to the end of the term. Come in being excited to learn stage light design and how you can implement these techniques in other aspects of your life.
Absolutely take this class if you are interested about the properties and qualities of light and how to actually use them effectively.
Take this course! One of the best courses I've taken at Harvard. This will change the way you think about light in everyday situations. Take this course if you're at all interested in photography, theater, color theory, design, architecture, or really any visual art.
This is an awesome class! It's fun, it's hands–on, and you'll get to meet new people and try new things. This class encourages you to go out of the Harvard arts scene, pay close attention to your everyday surroundings, and be creative.
