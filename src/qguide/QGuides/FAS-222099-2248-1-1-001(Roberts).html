What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Well organized class, I'm not in the architecture track and thoroughly enjoyed the discussions and the introduction to different methods
If you want to get the most out of this course really do the readings, be prepared to talk, and go to office hours! As someone on the Architecture Track I found this class insightful and exciting.
The readings vary in amount of time needed from week to week and are absolutely crucial for your experience in the class and discussions! The class size will be intimate which in my opinion really helps make the discussions more engaging and educational! I have really learned so much from this course and highly recommend because these topics are extremely foundational for architectural history and theory!!
If you have any interest in architecture, take this class! Will not sugarcoat it, the readings were usually long and dense, and needed to successfully complete the class. It was so worth it to me though. For me, I made this class a priority, so I don't know if that can apply to everyone but that was what I needed to do.
