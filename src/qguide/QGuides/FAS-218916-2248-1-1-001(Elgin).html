The prompts are quite tricky and require a lot of thought and attention to detail, so plan in advance and really engage with the readings.
Very well run, Elgin is a fantastic professor and offers great breadth and depth in the readings!
Take this class! Generally the class will task you to read a lot of philosophical texts, but they are interesting and the assignments aren't too hard to navigate.
