This is such a great class!! Even if you're not a heritage speaker and/or got bumped up from intermediate, you will do well. Ate Lady makes sure that all students feel comfortable speaking and gives lots of support and guidance.
I cannot recommend this class enough! Ate Lady is an incredible professor, and the smaller class sizes allows you to get to know her more personally. She caters the course content to your interests/concentrations and even invites Tagalog speakers from fields you are interested it which goes to show her efforts for her students to be engaged in the class and learn Tagalog. Not only that, because of the small class size, you are able to get to know your peers and form a tight knit community where I personally felt that it was okay to make mistakes. I would recommend this course if you are looking to take your Tagalog a step up as you will be reading research papers and deeper literature. But the assignments are fair, and she grades generously.
