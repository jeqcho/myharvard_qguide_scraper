I highly HIGHLY recommend this class. I took it as my intro to sociology and was not disappointed. The class dives into fascinating topics such as masculinity, the pay gap, women in sports, and more. Professor Viterna is so passionate and knowledgable and creates a welcoming environment in the class where everyone feels comfortable discussing controversial topics. The assignments are fun, too: a movie night essay, a research project, and a final op–ed. This class was the highlight of the semester and I would tell all my friends to take it.
