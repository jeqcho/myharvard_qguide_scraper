This class is awesome. Get ready for a great work out. The drums are transcendental.
Please take this course!! Jeffrey Page is one of the best professors. He will definitely push you (the warm–ups will be intense, but as someone trying to get in shape, they are doable), but it is so worth it. Learning about the history behind the dances we learn, as well as choreographing our own dances at the end of the course, really gives you a greater appreciation for the arts. Also, the live drummers at every other class really brings the class and the dances we are learning to life. Highly recommend!!
This is a physically challenging class but definitely worth the effort. Jeffery is a super passionate person that brings his energy to the room 100% and really connects with the space. He also sprinkles his words of wisdom about life and you really get 2 hours of quality time with yourself, letting go of the external stress from harvard life. The only downside may be that history–wise, it doesn't engage as much in depth as introduced in the course website. Mostly carries in form of weekly posts and a 20 minutes discussion in the beginning of class.
Honestly didn’t know I could take courses like this here, probably one of my favorite things I’ve done being here.
