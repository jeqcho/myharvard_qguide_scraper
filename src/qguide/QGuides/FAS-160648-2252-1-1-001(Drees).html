Comments
The community fostered in this course was one of the highlights of my semester. Every week I was in awe of my classmates' creativity, ideas, and the connections everyone would find between our different readings. I loved how many different types of artists I was exposed to throughout the course of the semester. Also, the New York trip is great!! Overall, I think this is a class where you get out of it what you put in. If you truly engage with the materials and your classmates, you will learn a lot, but it's up to you to really put in that work.
Be open–minded and ready to learn because this class will teach you a lot about the arts.
