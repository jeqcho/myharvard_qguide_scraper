She will cultivate a class community that really feels like a mini–family by the end!! If you care about writing and want to push yourself to get better, and you're willing to put in the time, you couldn't make a better decision. As a senior, her classes are the ones that have changed me the most.
This class is easily one of the best I've taken!! It's great for musicians and non–musician music–lovers alike!! You will think critically about what music actually means to you (do you think about it as an instrumental phenomenon, do you fixate on lyrics like they're poetry) and how to use it to better tell stories that are meaningful to you. My classmates formed a great community and provided really good feedback. This was an environment in which I felt comfortable and uplifted to tell personal stories and find my voice. Would highly highly recommend for both the aspiring author and journal entry–ist who wants to feel more authentic.
Please take this course. Great class. Melissa is an amazing instructor and the students are all extremely invested in the subject.
Melissa is one of my favorite professors at Harvard; all of her workshops are centered almost entirely around sharing and receiving feedback, and I cannot recommend her enough!!

– You will learn so much from your peers!

TAKE THIS WORKSHOP!!! Melissa is amazing. If you have any interest in creative writing, creative nonfiction is an amazing way to learn a LOT! The essays for this course were super fun, and I even got to perform a song I wrote for the course as one of my essays. The music lens for the course was innovative and perfect. I loved it so much and would 100% take it again if I could.
PLEASE PLEASE PLEASE take a creative writing course with Melissa. She is an incredible instructor and as soon as I was done with this one I was already on the lookout for another course I could take under her. She fosters very fruitful discussion and prevents the conversation veering off topic, is very funny and cares about her students and the work they write. This course was so cathartic, as we learned to write about music and moments in life that mean a lot to us in a way that other people can connect to. Feedback from peers was so useful and I learned so much about giving others constructive feedback with care.
I've never reflected on my own life in a class at Harvard as deeply as I have in this workshop.
If you have any interest at all in exploring your own life through creative nonfiction, please do yourself a favor and take this class. Melissa is one of the sweetest, most wholesome, and most talented people you will ever work with. She cares so deeply about her students, and works tirelessly to build the most supportive community around her. Her enthusiasm for the very craft of writing is infectious, and because of the nature of the course, you get to build such a strong and tight community with her and all of the other peers that are taking the course. If you need accommodations of any kind, Melissa will bend over backwards to make sure you have what you need, and if ever anything comes up throughout the semester, she will be right by your side to help in whatever way she can. The topics are fun to write about, the community that forms is amazing, and working with Melisa is an absolute joy. Take the class! You will not regret it.
