What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This is an essential course that demands a high level of critical thinking and explores the interplay between objective and subjective perspectives. Contemporary archaeology demands its participants to evaluate the concept of modernity and the accuracy of its proposed uniqueness, and it calls for a new sense of reflexivity and awareness that acknowledges with bias, working with full awareness rather than striving to eradicate it. This course pushes its students to question and articulate the goals, values, and priorities of archaeology, treating the discipline as one still very much in the process of defining itself.
This is a course you can feel safe to talk, to criticize and to reconstruct.
