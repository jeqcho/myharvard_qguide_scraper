What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Although this class is everyday and has two dictations/mini quizzes every week, I think you can gain a lot from it as long as you are diligent about learning the new vocabulary and grammar structures as they come. However, it is somewhat easy to get behind so make sure to review as much as you can to keep your Chinese sharp and ready to be used in class or for assignments.
Take this class!! It's so fun and you'll learn so much. The teaching staff is incredible, assignments are fair and engaging, and the class in general is so thorough while still being fun. The class moves really fast, so it's super important that you study consistently and stay on top of everything. I found Chinese bb much easier than Chinese ba, so if you took ba but are debating whether or not to take bb, you should take it! After getting the very basic foundation from ba, even though you're learning more complex grammar in bb, it feels more approachable.
I loved Chinese Ba and I loved Chinese Bb! The teaching staff is excellent – I love how enthusiastic they are and how they incorporate humor into the lessons. There are plenty of resources, from the textbook to Quizlet to the weekly grammar/vocab lists. They are also experimenting with a Chinese learning GPT assistant, which I didn't really use, but I heard was helpful.

Your grade is not majorly impacted by a single assignment or test, so make sure you're just showing up consistently and don't fall behind. If you do, they allow late submissions for homework. I found Quizlet and the textbook really helpful for prepping for dictation!
This course is such a great intro to Chinese with amazing teaching staff! Would definitely recommend.
