This class was an absolute trip, but the good outweighs the bad for the right type of person. I took this class as a prospective EE concentrator to test my interest in the different areas of EE. The course content is very overwhelming in its pace and without a section (which would have been life changing) it is very easy to fall behind. Fortunately, Gu and Reddi are extremely accessible and helpful in office hours and answered all of my questions, even basic ones, without making me feel stupid or incapable. My experience in office hours affirmed my decision to concentrate in EE because once so many challenging concepts made sense to me, I realized that I loved learning about EE (particularly the digital side) more than anything else I have ever learned and I found the challenge very satisfying to work through.
