Comments
The Self in German Idealism with Anne Dymek was one of the greatest classroom experiences of my life, she cultivates the best discussions and makes the most difficult texts fun and rewarding. I cannot recommend this course enough!
Awesome class. Immerse yourself in the readings and take your time––such a pleasure.
A seminar like no other––keep in mind this section was taught entirely in German and felt like a rigorous humanities course in a German university. It is difficult? Of course. But all you should focus on is getting as much out of the material as possible. I certainly did not understand all of the concepts we discussed––our wonderful lecturer, Anne, didn't either! That's part of the joy of the section. We all truly wanted to be there and would not rather be in any other place, and the enthusiasm really showed. I haven't written my final project yet, but I know there's so much content I can use that we've learned that I want to include in it. I am so excited for that culmination of the course.

As a side note, if you can ever take a class with Anne, please do––it's unlike anything else you've ever learned. She has such an eccentric, unique style that makes every section a true delight. There's so many stories of her life you can try to tease out of her, from her stay in Afghanistan to her time at the Sorbonne.
