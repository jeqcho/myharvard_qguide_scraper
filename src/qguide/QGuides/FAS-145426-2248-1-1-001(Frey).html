Comments
take this class! It's absolutely amazing and such a cool experience. You learn a lot about how to write (mostly by your own exploration) and it's a cool way to see where your strengths and weaknesses are with non–fiction writing and get to read a lot of cool writing as well. The workshop element is also super cool and unique and a great way to see how your writing is perceived and understood and learn more about how to improve on it
This course is the best course I did during my time here at Harvard. If you are lucky enough to get in then enjoy.
Loved this class. I don't consider myself a writer at all, it's just something I enjoy and wanted to learn more about and I really loved this class. Darcy is super knowledgeable and makes workshopping less nerve wracking. People really put in a lot of work into their essays and I learned a lot from the other students as well – we all came from different levels of writing experience which was great.
this is a fantastic course for anyone, but especially for inexperienced writers. you will rarely get the chance to receive such in–depth, meaningful feedback on your writing. and darcy is a kind, considerate, and personal professor. take the class!
This is one of my favorite classes, if not my favorite class, that I have ever taken at any institution. Darcy is so knowledgeable and generous with his experiences with writing, and I found it particularly helpful to learn techniques and tools through the published work of other writers. I have become a more discerning reader through our discussions in this class, and it has inspired me to get back to writing more often. The workshop was extremely well run and well paced. It was a good amount of work (reading, writing, and providing feedback), but it was also almost always the first work I tackled in any given week. Everything was just that enjoyable. I cannot more highly recommend this workshop, take it!!!
