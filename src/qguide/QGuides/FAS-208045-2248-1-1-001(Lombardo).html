I highly recommend ES105 to anyone. Everything you need to know is taught to you during the onboarding process. The class is also like a family and we all have a lot of fun together. The work is impactful, relevant, and interesting.
I highly recommend this course to anyone who wants to step out of their academic bubble and work on a real world project. It's a lot of fun!
This class is amazing. You will learn a lot about whichever software you work with (Revit, Civil3D, etc). I loved being able to learn new software, apply my engineering knowledge in a real world situation, and be able to help a community!
If you want to get involved with work that can make a difference in people's lives, then EWB is a great place to turn to! There's a lot of practical real–world experience that can be gained, and the community is lovely. That being said, there is a considerable amount of effort that goes into these projects, so just something to keep in mind when considering this as a fifth course.
This class is structured a little differently since it's a two–credit class. Instead of homework, you have weekly milestones you need to submit (this is essentially your homework). They're not bad, but they're always team–based so just make sure you're communicating with your team about distributing and getting work done.
Take this course if you are passionate about the work and going to be an engineer in the future. Or if you want to meet incredibly passionate people, because EWB is a great community. Otherwise assignments can seem mundane at times.
You learn a lot in this course about implementing designs in the real world! You get to use skills learned from other classes and actually apply them. It has a great team environment and I would definitely recommend taking this course!
Take this course!!! Best part of my college experience. Very unique and rewarding
TAKE IT!! EWB is such an awesome class community, and people here are super smart, dedicated, and kind.
