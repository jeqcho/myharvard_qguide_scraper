Take this course if interested in Latin at all! The course was extremely well organized and taught exceptionally well. It was clear that Ivy has an impressive grasp of the course content and was able to effectively teach complex concepts, while also managing to keep the class engaging. I really appreciate all the effort put in for the games / slides / fun activities throughout the course. Hannah was an amazing TF who was extremely effective at teaching us more advanced translation best tactics and its clear that she cares a lot about the students. I found her advice and teaching to be extremely useful and it made me a much better student. Also really appreciated the enthusiasm she brought to each class.
Honestly, if you are thinking of taking Latin because it'll be easy, think again. It wasn't the homework that was hard, it was the language –– which isn't anyone's fault except for the Romans. Wtf were they thinking?
IF YOU SENSE YOU ARE FALLING BEHIND, SEEK HELP IMMEDIATELY. I only passed this class thanks to the help of the TF Hannah, and endless cramming of material that had been swept up in the quick pace of the class. If you must take Latin or are linguistically gifted, learn your damn declensions, cases, and vocab. The exams are not too bad and make up a majority of your grade.
Take this course! Teaching staff is great and not hard to keep up in this class.
Ivy is a magnificent teacher. She is amazing at teaching Latin, so sweet, and helpful. The course materials are great, too.
