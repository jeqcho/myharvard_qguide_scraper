This course is great! The content is certainly challenging, but do remember that you can retake all the quizzes (well, if they keep that component of the syllabus) and that participation counts for a large part of the grade. So do not let the challenge deter you from taking the course. If you have an interest in this subject, this course will be great for you! Not sure who will teach it in the future but Professor Truscott is really nice and super available in office hours for help etc so yeah overall this course is a big RECOMMEND!
Be patient and study a lot at the beginning. It can be frustrating but there is no way to learn the all the conjugations without swallowing the pill and memorizing . Once you have it down, the course gets a lot easier, I promise! The instructor is very patient and willing to help her students so don't be afraid!

I also think Classical Japanese is deeply important to anyone doing Japanese studies. If you are a debating on taking this class, I highly recommend it!
Professor Truscott is so helpful if you reach out. The grammar can get super challenging (so much memorization!), so make sure you hit the ground running; review the material every week to stay on top of the new grammar.
