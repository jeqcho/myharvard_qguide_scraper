Patrick is great and very supportive
*Probably the best tutorial to take. It's interesting and the readings arent bad, prob 15–20 PDF pages per class of reading. Patrick has a weird teaching style I guess but he is overall willing to g the extra mile and meet with you and make sure you're staying on track.
Do the readings.
For an Ec970 tutorial, this class is *amazing*. The professor expects about 2 hours of reading per class period (so about 4 hours of reading a week). The coding problem sets are a pain and feel like a drag on the course. I don't think I learned anything from them, but the writing assignments were amazing.
Though this might be considered one of the more challenging sections, Patrick spends a lot of time with each student and pushes them individually to do their best. He offers a lot of time outside of class to meet with people 1:1, so I definitely learned a lot about the topic as well as shortfalls in my writing. The topic of housing and homelessness is one that most people should know and learn about, since it is definitely more nuanced than I initially thought.
