I enjoyed this course significantly more than Math Ma. TRY TO GET SARAH AS YOUR SECTION LEADER. Sarah actually teaches concepts and has the perfect balance of instruction and group work. Try to complete problem sets early. Exams are very fair and similar to practice exams. Concepts are not too difficult and easily mastered with practice. As long as you stay on top of the content, you will be good.
Take this class only if you have a concentration/secondary requirement OR are planning to take Math 1B. There is no other reason to take this class. It does not have major practical applications. There are three psets a week that will take at least 2 hours to complete. This class also has skill checks and two mid–term exams. If you want to be successful in this course, I would highly recommend going to MQC a few times a week, especially if you have no calc background besides Math MA. The textbook is really not necessary to study for the exams, provided you complete most/all of the practice exams. Try to get a good CA if you can as well, I had a Doug and he was great! Would recommend.
