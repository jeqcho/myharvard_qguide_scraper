What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
I enjoyed this class. Professor Egorova is excellent and is willing to help you whenever. She is extremely accessible during office hours and wants you to succeed. The quizzes/exams are fair and she is flexible with the homework. I feel that I learned a lot leaving the first semester, especially as an "intro" course.
Great class for heritage speakers! Class section is super chill but over time you will see that your grammar and spelling improves leaps and bounds!! Also the department throws fun events that you can go to with great home food. If you grew up speaking Russian in the US, take this class no matter what, you will learn so much and it doesn't matter where you start. Reach out to the professor to discuss and most likely this is the place for you!
This class is truly amazing. If you're a heritage speaker, I cannot reccomend it enough. Professor Egorova is so freaking amazing and cares about all of her students so deeply. She is so cool and full of awesome stories, and really loves to teach. She has a great sense of humor and loves to joke with her students. I cannot say enough good things about her and this course.
If you are a heritage speaker who wants to gain confidence in their spoken and written Russian, absolutely take this class. It is one of the best courses I have taken so far––Veronika Aleksandrovna is a fantastic teacher, and the community you form around being heritage speakers and meeting regularly is lovely. Know that no matter your level, if you can speak some Russian, understand it fairly well, and are willing to learn and put in effort, this is the course for you. Since it only runs every two years, do it at your earliest opportunity––you won't regret it.
