This class is fun, engaging, and provides the perfect amount of Spanish knowledge to eavesdrop on people speaking Spanish in public.
I really enjoyed this class, and will be taking Spanish 20 next semester even though I only planned to fulfill my language requirement. I was surprised that I enjoyed this class, because I didn't like Spanish 10 very much. This class is very similar to Spanish 10, but I had a better idea of what I was getting into, and I did a bunch of Duolingo over winter break. That helped a lot I think, and I recommend doing the same if you are worried.
This class has a bit of busy work, definitely more than Spanish 10. That said, it meets only three days a week compared to Spanish 10's four, and doesn't have a written final exam. This is one of those classes you unfortunately just have to take to meet the requirement. Enroll in the same section as your friends, as the boring nature of this class never goes away.
Good class, structure is well spread out but honestly more work than I had expected.
