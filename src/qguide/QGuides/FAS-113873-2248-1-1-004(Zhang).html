Take this class! You need to put the work in, but if you do not only will you get a good grade you'll be able to speak Chinese!! Don't be afraid of this course, the teachers make it super approachable and offer so much help and guidance. If you have any desire to learn Chinese, please take this class!
I definitely recommend taking this course if you are interested in Chinese language or culture. I would not recommend it for those looking for an easy language course to fill your requirement because it does take up a lot of time, especially if you have no background in Chinese. I did and at points found it a little difficult. The teaching team is really supportive and understanding though. Not too much work but be ready for mini quizzes (dictations) every Tuesday/Thursday on material you haven’t learned before (read textbook).
