This class is a must take! It was an eye–opening course that enables you to learn a lot of diverse perspectives for any given topic covered in class.
You should be so lucky to gain a spot in this course! This was easily one of my favorite classes of my entire college career. Professor Lajer–Burcharth is brilliant in both her ability to curate a syllabus and in guiding discussion. I left every class feeling intellectually excited by the material and our conversation about it. This is not an easy class; with three presentations and pretty rigorous readings, you will need to pay attention and contribute meaningfully, but the payoff will be absolutely worth it. I felt like I learned so much. Professor Lajer–Burcharth also puts so much effort into bringing art to the classroom and bringing the classroom to where the art is (Paris!!!). Getting to examine so many incredible works of art was part of what made this course fantastic. Be prepared to work hard but to enjoy every minute of it.
Take this class!!!!! It was one of my favorites in my time at Harvard. Professor Lajer–Burcharth cares deeply about both the students and the material, and it shows. She makes an effort to get to know everyone individually, and it's also clear that she has prepared thoroughly for every class. She is very interested in the discussions and does a great job of leading them productively. The content itself is also very interesting –– even though the topic might seem somewhat limited, you learn quickly that it's actually quite expansive. And you get to dye your own scarf with cochineal and go to Paris!!!
Absolutely 100% without question take this class!! We had such a great group, and while the class trip helped with bonding, even our discussions beforehand were incredibly fun and engaging. Professor Lajer–Burcharth is so brilliant, as is everyone else who took the class with me, and it made for just an exceptional experience.

Yes, we went to Paris, but even without that I'd still say that this turned out to be one of my favorite classes I've taken while at Harvard. The readings, discussions, and assignments all came together so beautifully to form basically the perfect class. Highly recommend.
This class was one of my favorites I have taken at Harvard. The class trip was amazing and allowed the students to bond, but also was so insightful to the course material. I could not recommend more taking a class with Professor Lajer–Burcharth; she is knowledgeable and all of her feedback in and out of class is useful and constructive. She leads the class extremely well and makes us feel comfortable with the material and sharing our opinions. She treats the material with care and it is evident she is passion about these topics, which is infectuous. The course is structured wonderfully and we get to look at different material each week.
This is a must–take course. Professor Lajer–Burcharth is so knowledgeable and kind, always willing to provide in–depth feedback. The course is well–structured and never feels like a 3–hour class, with visits other professionals, class discussions, and close–looking at art. The material of the course was of course pertinent and deeply–interesting, and Professor Lajer–Burcharth facilitates an amazing classroom environment that fosters and encourages such discussions. Definitely one of my favorite classes!
