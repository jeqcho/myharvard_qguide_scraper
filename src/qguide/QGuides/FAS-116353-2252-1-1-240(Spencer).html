Exactly what you'd expect given the course description. Interesting material, with a nice balance of reading and films. Not super intense grading, but not as automatic an A as some other courses from what I've heard. If I had to do it all over again I'd still take this class.
This is an interesting class that's not too difficult. The title is a bit misleading since we don't really talk about the "science" of science fiction, but nevertheless the class contains some great discussions about science fiction.
Please take this. Best expos ever. Chill class, great writing experience. Perfect, just try hard.
This course is extremely interesting, and the writing is mostly based on literary analysis. If that's not your thing, maybe reconsider. Otherwise, it will be fun for you!
If you're interested in Science Fiction, I recommend taking this class. The discussions, readings, and assignments are interesting. But beware, long discussions take most of class and because of the Expos required format, some of the essays feel unnecessary (meaning readings and essay prompts are unnecessarily convoluted). Dr. Spencer is kind and willing to review feedback, but it can take a while to get assignments back. I would recommend!
Great topic, very engaging! Not too much reading compared to other expos and good guidance.
Will you learn about the "Science" of Science Fiction? No. You will, however, learn about the science of low essay grades.
