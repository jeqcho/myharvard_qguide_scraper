What I recommend is mainly flashcards and practice questions. For each learning pod and reading section, you can download the slides and ask ChatGPT 4.0 (Harvard gives this to you for free) to make you anki flashcards based on the slides. You can then ask GPT to save these as a .csv file and upload these to Anki. Then, do these flashcards daily and constantly add more cards from the upcoming learning pods. Make sure to do the Anki cards for the respective learning pods before each lecture so you can have a clue about what's going on. In class, download the lecture slides on your iPad onto Goodnotes and annotate the slides on your iPad as the lecture progresses so you understand what's happening.

For PIEs, make sure to study the practice PIE questions and mock PIE. Make notes of your mistakes and repeat missed questions.
