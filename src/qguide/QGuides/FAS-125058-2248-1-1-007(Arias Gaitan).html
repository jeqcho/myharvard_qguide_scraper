I would tell future students to take this class if they want to take a beginner level Spanish class.
This is a fantastic course, and Professor Arteta is an incredible individual. He is a great instructor and a very kind person.
This is such a chill course. If you’ve taken Spanish before, you’ll be fine. It reviews the basics and I think the hw and tests are quite fair so long as you study the vocab and conjugations. Before turning in projects or reports, go to office hours to review it with your professor to fine tune it. Also, the talkabroads can be a little annoying but prepare a lot of small questions to ask, ask them to elaborate, if you think they’re saying something that you wouldn’t understand looking back then ask them to repeat it or say it slower to make your life easier when you’re trying to transcribe the convo to write your report. And make sure to participate in class!
This is a great course. Professor Arteta is the greatest Spanish teacher I've ever had. He's so knowledgeable and does a great job explaining the history, syntax and structure of the language.
