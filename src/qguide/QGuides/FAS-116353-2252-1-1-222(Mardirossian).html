Taleen is amazing. Expos was my favorite class this semester; Taleen's radient energy and support make it impossible to not enjoy the class.
This is the best Expos 20 to take. Taleen was the most amazing preceptor I could ask for. The readings were short/few in number, yet extraordinarily engaging. The discussions we had in class were truly productive– I came out of class energized and excited about what I learned every time. I wasn't looking forward to Expos, but this is one of my favorite classes I took my first year. Taleen can be a bit strict (which should be expected in a small class with all first years), but is incredibly sweet. Her feedback comes quickly and is incredibly in–depth. If you need help, she is always willing to listen and offer advice. There are only two major papers for this course– a comparative essay and a research essay. You have PLENTY of time and support to write these. Every other first–year taking Expos would have deadlines 1–2 weeks before I did, but the extra days really improved the quality of my writing. Regarding the topic, if you've had any interest in language (not just foreign, but English itself), understanding, speaking, storytelling, whatever –– this is the class to take. I am leaving this semester with so many soft skills (regarding analysis in language) that I didn't have before. I cannot recommend this course enough. Everyone was thoroughly engaged and participated, and that's what made it amazing –– if you take this class, please participate! Taleen gives so much to us students, and if you give it back each discussion will have you leaving with a new incredible insight.

I'll remember this Expos class as one of my favorite courses for the rest of my time at Harvard.
THIS IS THE BEST EXPOS CLASS! I thoroughly enjoyed Tongue–Tied with Taleen. I gained really strong writing skills, and the course widened my view of all the ways in which language has a profound influence on our world. The essays were super manageable and enjoyable to write (especially the unit 2 essay). Taleen is super approachable and sweet, and the dynamic of the class is chill and fun from day one. She provides super detailed, thorough feedback on essays and is a very fair grader. All of us in the class also grew close to one another because of the small size of the class, and it was so nice to see our friendships transcend the classroom. A very rewarding experience.
Taleen is actually SO GOATED. Like genuinely. The readings yes are a little confusing from the start, but the fact that those are the hardest readings out of the entire semester is actually so nice. The comments that Taleen will leave on your paper is exactly what she expects you to fix, so as logn as you conference with her and elaborate on your ideas, she will provide an amazing grade on your paper. I think the main thing is that you cant expect an A on your paper without constant feedback. Continuously expressing your concerns in the class and underestanding what your weaknesses are will show Taleen a greater effort when you finally submit that essay.
