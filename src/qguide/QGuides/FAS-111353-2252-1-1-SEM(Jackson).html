What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Don't be intimidated if you are not familiar with music theory! It is a great class and you'll learn a lot from Professor Jackson, Fabian, and your classmates.
Music 4 is a fantastic course to get you started with composition, and it truly is designed to accomodate people with very different backgrounds/experience levels in music.
Professor Jackson and Fabian are absolutely wonderful and their feedback is very thoughtful and helpful. Can't recommend this course enough!
This class was a wonderful opportunity to be able to compose your own piece for the Parker Quartet, an amazing professional group. The class itself made a great environment for composing, which I’d never done before and found very rewarding. There were some logistical difficulties where the instructions for assignments were not entirely clear but I know Fabian mentioned some improvements for last year. I didn’t find the lectures super interesting but I understand that it could be difficult to meet everyone’s musical interests. Workshopping people’s compositions in section was my favorite part rather than practicing ear training but again I think it depends on what people want to get out of the class.
This is a really excellent course for all those who would like to be introduced to composition, develop their skills and a musician, and it has an amazing capstone project.
