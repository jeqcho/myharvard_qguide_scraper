It's a pleasure to learn from Andrei. This class is a rare opportunity to get hand held through the process of ideating, executing, and writing an economics paper.
Try to have some ideas for a project before the class begins. A lot of them won't work for reasons that Shleifer will come up with, and you need backup plans so that you can get the ball rolling sooner rather than later.
If you're interested in econ or thinking about writing an economics paper, I think this is one of the best classes you can take. The teaching staff is incredibly involved... meeting with Andrei, Simon, and Aakaash was honestly the best part of the course. You can meet with them up to three times a week if you want. They give a ton of thoughtful, helpful feedback on your work.

The weekly political economics topics are also more interesting than you might expect, and by the end, you've written a full paper and been part of a really fun, engaged group.
This is one of the best courses I've ever taken at Harvard. Andrei is really a gem, and his commitment to all his students is unmatched. He is such an advocate of exploration and really gives you free will on the paper. Plus, he is such a great mentor! Take this course if you have the chance.
This was potentially the best class I have taken at Harvard. For someone who's serious about economics, this class and 1011a are next to none in gathering a community of people who care about the field beyond a career in the world of finance. The weekly readings aren't that serious to actually do, but are worthwhile especially for the guest lectures. Class itself is great, Andrei is highly respected for a reason and all the topics are interesting and engaging, but the bulk of the class is the project. Be prepared to work and work hard since you can't mail this in. Shleifer is big on finding "interesting correlations", which essentially means finding a niche or heretofore unexplored dataset and finding something worthwhile in that dataset. If you're going to take the class, you might as well try your hardest to come up with something cool, especially since everyone in the class—likely due to selection bias—does a really good job. If you're thinking about taking the class, definitely apply and see what happens. If you're able to take the class, you will not regret doing it.
This class is amazing because Andrei is amazing — that goes without saying. Moreover, he does a great job of constructing a group of students who are all very smart and motivated, and we all became friends throughout the semester, supporting each other and growing together along the way.
This is an amazing class, which I would absolutely recommend if you're very excited about economics. The important thing is the research paper. Professor Shleifer is hugely energizing to work on research with—you get fantastic guidance and are encouraged to think about problems with a lot of ambition; based on what is interesting rather than what is untouched in the field. This class also attracts great peers, whom I would encourage getting to know. The prerequisites are accurate—you really don't need a background in macro/metrics, and much of the research process is centered around finding/working with new data. Papers are functionally optional to read but the four books are not. Definitely apply!
a wonderful, wonderful class you should absolutely take if you have a chance! Lectures are a survey of fascinating range of topics––I learned so much about everything from economics and the law to developmental econ and democracy. Outside of the lectures, there are so many people from the wonderful two TFs we had this year to previous students in the class who will support you in looking into anything you might be interested in. They will help you every step of the way from ideation to methodology to understanding and building on your results. Everyone ended up with interesting and well thought out projects—highly, highly recommend!! You'll see why I and lots of other people see this course as the absolute best Harvard has to offer!
Best course I have taken at Harvard, the people you will get to work with are truly amazing.
This is the best class at Harvard without a doubt. Be curious, connect with Professor Shleifer, and work hard to figure something out.
Best academic experience I have had at Harvard. If you have the chance, take this class!
