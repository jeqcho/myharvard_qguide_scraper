This is the best course for people who have no music experience who want to take a music class. You'll learn things that you can actually apply to the music you make on your own time.
Take this class!! It was lots of fun, and definitely very friendly to beginners. The teaching team is also outstanding for this course. Liam is an excellent lecturer, and my TF Sara was also wonderful. You get the chance to write a few songs in the class, which was honestly such a fun learning experience. The final exam was also very reasonable, and the teaching team provided lots of support and resources leading up to it (although in my opinion the course is already taught extremely well so there was really no need to study much!) I had such a wonderful experience in this class so you should totally consider taking it if you were ever interested in learning more about music!
