I'd say this course was absolutely incredible, with some caveats. The main draw of this class for me was reading the actual theories themselves, who are constantly being cited + thrown around in a lot in other courses; at least for my tutorial, we had really different things we brought to the test, and it felt like a really nice environment to be kind to each other while also voicing out our disagreements with the theories/each other. In that vein, I LOVE the reading list of 10b, with Nietzsche, Foucault, and Fanon being highlights for me; BUT I really wish we read Souls of Black Folk (which is so much better and important in terms of understanding double consciousness) instead of Black Reconstruction, the latter of which was quite boring/tedious to get through.

The main flaw of the course for me, however, were the lectures.
