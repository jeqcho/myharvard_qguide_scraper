Comments
This was just a fantastic pre–thesis–writing exprience, and definitely gave me more of a handle on both what I want to spend the next year of my life writing about and how I'll go about doing it. Professor Mitra is so incredibly supportive and kind and brilliant–––she will definitely push you to think and grow, and you will definitely thank her–––and Jelani is the same. I'm very grateful that I got to learn from them both :)
I LOVE Professor Mitra, and the class environment was lovely. She gives wonderful feedback and really made me feel comfortable reaching out for help. Aside from that, the reading assignments are manageable, but the final paper is very time consuming. I wish I would've known to start reading books for my final paper way early on in the semester. I didn't realize just how time consuming trying to read primary sources on top of other readings and paper deadlines would be, so post–spring break felt like such a time crunch. There also wasn't much in–class discussion about trying to narrow down topics, which I think would've been incredibly useful.
