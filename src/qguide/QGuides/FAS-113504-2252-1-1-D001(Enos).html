This class is fun! There are a lot of readings, but you do really learn a lot by engaging with them. The assignments are quite easy, so anything beyond them for learning is on you.
Gov 97 is such a weird class and isn't structured enough. Matthew is a great teacher to have, though, and he's a good choice. Public opinion is a very interesting and topical issue, so that helped make it more worthwhile. But in truth this class is just like 2 hours of discussing the readings.
Sophomore tutorial is a big learning curve, but essential if you want to pursue any kind of government research or work with any social scientist. I really enjoyed the structure of the course, and I thought it did the necessary hand–holding while giving independence to pursue a wide variety of topics. Personally, the subject of my tutorial really resonated with me, but I know that for some people, this is hit or miss. Grading is also hit–or–miss because of the section leader.
