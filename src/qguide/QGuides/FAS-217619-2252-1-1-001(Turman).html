This class is a boot camp for the French language, especially reading and writing. The professor is super organized in each class and covers many topics inside the classroom. It is an excellent course to practice and develop your French skills. However, you must be aware of the amount of work it requires. For each session, you could have a 2–page (single–spaced) reading, between 5 and 7 questions requiring answers of a couple of paragraphs, and grammar exercises. And because the class meets two times a week, you can have this amount of work twice weekly. These exercises helped students to improve and accelerate their language skills, but it is prudent to consider the time you need to spend in this class when designing your semester schedule.

The materials are diverse and interesting. There are extracts of French literature, videos, a film, and documentaries. You also have the flexibility to study the material of your choice in depth in the daily homework.

Quality matters for grading. I advise you to spend quality time on your assignments, and you will be okay. Just because the homework is a short reading does not mean that you don't need to provide in–depth analysis.
An amazing class! You will learn so much and grow as a writer & speaker! Super interesting content as well! Highly recommend
