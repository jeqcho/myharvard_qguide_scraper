This class is perfect for those seriously building a startup and hoping to enter an ecosystem of equally curious and driven peers. You get out of it what you put into it. It's really nice to get course credit for work that you'd be doing regardless on your startup.
This is not a course where you sit back and theorize about startups—it’s a hands–on, action–heavy experience that will force you to test your assumptions early and often. Be ready to get out of your comfort zone, talk to people (a lot of them!), and embrace the discomfort of ambiguity. You do not need to have a tech background or a perfect startup idea on day one. What matters most is having the curiosity to learn from users and the resilience to pivot when your assumptions get crushed—which they will. The class can be especially rewarding if you come with an open mind and a willingness to learn by doing.
