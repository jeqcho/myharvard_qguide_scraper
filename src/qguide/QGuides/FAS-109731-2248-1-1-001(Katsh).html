Gabe is the best. He is an exceptional professor. This course is designed so that it is not all consuming, but you get so much out of each and every reading and each and every seminar meeting. Gabe has clearly put immense thought into the design and structure of this course and it makes for a really awesome learning experience. Gabe also facilitates discussion in a way so that you are constantly learning new things and the conversations never feel dull or boring. I cannot recommend this course enough!
This class gives you a fantastic knowledge of the healthcare system in the U.S. as well as comparatively to all other developed nations in the world that have a form of universal healthcare. Gabe's facilitation of discussions made space to ensure everyone had the opportunity to speak and contribute. He really took the time to ensure everyone had a solid understanding of the difficult philosophical concepts and healthcare insurance intricacies before moving on and building on those topics, something often find rare in most courses. The readings and course materials were all super fascinating and thought–provoking and made for great reflection papers and class discussions. Gabe's interactive in–class activities such as examining health insurance plans also helped provide deeper insight into the real life implications of these concepts. As long as you prepare and study for the midterm and put in the proper effort one can expect to do well. Gabe is also great about making himself available outside of class for any discussions or further questions on course material or assignments. I would enthusiastically recommend this course to any gov concentrator looking for clarity and comprehensiveness in learning tricky political theory concepts and healthcare policy and ethics.
I loved this course! This course will teach you the fundamental principles behind healthcare ethics, and you will start to see how ubiquitous these ethical dilemmas come up in our healthcare system today.
Really interesting course materials. Gabe is a great professor. The exam is hard, and considering GOV 94s normally do not have exams, if this component scares you I would not take this course. Super interesting topics covered across the course. You will leave with a good understanding of different ethical concepts and how they conflict with health care, as well as the health care system. Wish we covered the readings and challenges of the health care industry more, felt like we were rushing sometimes. Make sure if you take this class you are good with exams. An understanding of ethics would be helpful before taking it.
TAKE THIS CLASS! YOU WILL NEVER REGRET IT! ALL HAIL OUR LORD AND SAVIOR GABE
take this class! Gabe is such an amazing teacher, and the class conversations are really interesting and easy to engage with. The readings are super doable and very easy to understand, and this class has made me think of healthcare so differently
This course was one of the best classes I have taken at Harvard. It is a must take for any student interested in being a healthcare provider or working in the healthcare industry. Gabe did a great job balancing lecturing and promoting insightful discussion. I enjoyed discussing the material each week, and found it fun to apply the principles we learned to real world scenarios and ethical questions in the healthcare industry. This class definitely requires a bit of reading each week, but it is well worth it and I found readings to be interesting and thought provoking.
Take this class if you can!! Gabe is an amazing instructor and genuinely cares about his students. Class discussions are usually pretty interesting and the activities during class time really help you learn the material.
