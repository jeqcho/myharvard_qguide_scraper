Spanish50 is a chill way to get better at your Spanish. Having just taken Spanish 40 was enough to be successful in the class!
Take this class!! Spanish 40 was really meh for me but Spanish 50 is phenomenal. For exams, you write and act out your own play with a group. This intimidated me at first but it's actually SO MUCH FUN– I've never ever acted before and I'm really really glad I got the opportunity to try it out in a low–stakes setting while generating whatever hilarious story I wanted. The homework is around 10 questions, takes maybe ten minutes. There are points where you suddenly have to read an entire act of a play in two days but if you look ahead and plan for it then it's not a big deal. Super chill, super fun, highly recommend. And I got to interact with other students without that stifling awkwardness that sections usually have. My section only had 8 students and every TF is phenomenal anyway.
Take this class for spanish citacion with Ruy! He was super supportive, provided great feedback on written works and very smart. Its a little more unstructured than past spanish classes and get ready to write and perform plays. That is the bulk of the exams, so if you don't like performing, dont take this spanish class.
Don't hesitate! Just take it
