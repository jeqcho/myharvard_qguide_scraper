Comments
Landoni's behavioral finance tutorial is AWESOME. The material is extremely interesting and Landoni is an excellent teacher. It is clear that he is excited about the material and is always extremely well–prepared for class. He is a unique professor, and I mean that in a good way. The right amount quirky!

His grading is clear and he always gives lots of feedback. Make sure to do the readings and start your final project early! TAKE THIS CLASS! Definitely one of the best tutorials.
It's not going to be an easy A especially since there are so many mini–tasks. Lots of reading.
This is a good course – Mattia does care about the material and truly tries to get everyone to engage with the content. I also liked how the assignments were structured so that you slowly write a draft of each part of your final paper so by the end, all you have to do is edit extensively rather than write a whole final paper from scratch.

However, the course was not perfect (at least for me). I felt like we would really be getting into the weeds of the theory and econometrics of papers that didn't even go into it themselves. I found myself struggling to remember parts of metrics that were useful to participate and therefore not being able to contribute. I think it would have been more useful to dive deeper into the data portions of the papers and analyze those. Also, this class is NOT an easy A by any means. Yes, tutorial is supposed to be hard. I do think if you need an A, you should look into other courses. That said, this class is pretty interesting and if you are willing to grind and like behavioral econ, I recommend it.
