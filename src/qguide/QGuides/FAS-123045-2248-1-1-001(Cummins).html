This class is difficult if you don't go above and beyond yourself. The instructor is not the best, so you have to put in effort on your own to get something meaningful out of this course.
While no doubt a difficult language, this class makes learning Sanskrit as easy as possible and equally rewarding. The course does an excellent job of guiding students through their language journey but a considerable amount of additional work is necessary to actually internalize the language and the grammar – even beyond what it takes to get an A.
The erring feeling that learning is a process of perpetual reminiscence will dance with you for life. Choose what you learn wisely. And this course teaches you how to choose wisely, for it would disclose a blade–edge layer of value over things that you dont perceive without getting yourself acquainted with the unique Sanskrit signscapethe beautifully maddening traces of infinity itself that glows at the limits of linguistic generativity.
–Be prepared for a heavy workload, as it's designed to accelerate progress into deeper reading, but the payoff is worth it!
Take this class but make sure to put the necessary work in as it is easy to fall behind. If you put ample work in though, it is so rewarding!
