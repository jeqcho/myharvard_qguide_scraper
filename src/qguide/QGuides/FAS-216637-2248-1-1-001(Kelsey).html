TAKE HUM90. If you do one thing at Harvard let it be HUM90. It is without a doubt the best class I've taken at Harvard. Lauren and Robin work tirelessly to create a productive learning environment, one that fosters creativity and growth for all of its students. Moreover, their careful feedback on assignments will not only improve your writing but also help you grow as a person.
Take this class! Definitely one of the best decisions I made sophomore year. Took this class just for fun but exceeded my expectations. Robin and Lauren were always so supportive and funny. You will also meet really smart and not insufferable peers unlike some other humanities classes. The wide coverage of topics, interesting assignments, and group outings (concert, romeo and juliet, movie screening) made this class even more enjoyable. Essays are not too bad: 500 words every other week. Final project also allowed a lot of creativity to conclude the class.
While the future remains largely uncertain, there is a prediction I can make with full confidence. In 10 or 15 years, I will look back to HUM 90 and think: Yes—that class changed the trajectory of my life.
If you, like my past self, are a STEM concentrator increasingly curious about non–scientific fields, I urge you to take HUM 90. You might, like my present self, come out of your sophomore fall concentrating in the humanities.
Lauren and Robin are fantastic. They make every week feel like you are a family hosting a special guest.
