What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This course completely changed how I approach my work as a musician. I would highly recommend that others take this course. Keep in mind that it requires a lot of work and thought in order to succeed but if you are willing to put in the effort it is well worth it.
This is a difficult class with a very specific niche –– the whole class is an introduction to the software Max/MSP, which is different from most other DAWs. That said, I would highly recommend the course. It gave me a great perspective on a different kind of music–making that I hadn't been exposed to before. For music concentrators, a class like this is an awesome way to experience music outside of the typical music–major trajectory.
TAKE THIS CLASS!! Best music class I've taken at Harvard. It's a beautiful synergy of learning a new music language (Max MSP) and also becoming better as a musician and performer itself. Hans deeply cares about his students and will give back the energy that you give to him. Victor was an awesome TF as well and made sure to spend hours of time with multiple students outside of class to get our Max patches sounding great.
I strongly encourage you to take this course if you wish to expand your view on how music should be played/what music is. The teaching staff is incredibly helpful and wise, and the course truly shaped my view of music as a listener and creator.
