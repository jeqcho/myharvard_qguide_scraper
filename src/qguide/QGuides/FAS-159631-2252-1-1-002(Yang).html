Definitely take this class if you want to take your Chinese to the next level!
Chinese never fails to be my favorite course every single semester, and 130 is no different. I think it is very on par to the difficulty of CHNSE 120 (vocab/grammar is definitely more difficult than 120's still, but class itself is similar in difficulty). The teachers have always been phenomenal and it is so absolutely worth taking this course if you are looking for your citation. A little review every day goes a long way, the Quizlets are always up to date, grammar sheets are super helpful, all is good.
Amazing class with amazing lecturers. Spend time on the quizlets
If you have the chance, take this course! It can definitely be a time commitment with the bi–weekly quizzes and daily walk to Vanserg, but the teaching team is absolutely phenomenal and will make getting through the semester much more doable. Office hours are during odd hours of the day, which can make them difficult to attend, but professors are very attentive towards student concerns and learning.
The Chinese language program at Harvard is amazing! I've always had great experiences in their courses, and this one was no exception. Be prepared for a pretty fast pace, but as long as you keep up with weekly quizzes you will be fine
