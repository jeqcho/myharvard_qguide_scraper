What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Take this class if you want an introduction to how economists view government policy. It's a gem and you'll learn a lot.
I would be cautious taking this course as a theory pre–requisite. The exams and PSETs were overall pretty fair, but it seemed like at times they were just testing our rote memorization of the material as opposed to our ability to actually apply it (e.g., with the True/False questions).
This class is moreso a combination of econometrics and micro than it is what I imagined a "designing government policy" class to be like. It is still interesting, but there is less of a focus on how policy is designed and more a focus on research design and broad–scale policy applications.
