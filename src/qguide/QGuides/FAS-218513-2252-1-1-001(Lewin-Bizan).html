Great class, great instructor.
This course was amazing! The material covered is very relevant and will inform the way that you think about many of the most pressing issues facing children today. Dr. Lewin–Bizan is an excellent professor, who knows so much about these topics and is very kind and engaging. In addition, the readings, lectures, and class discussions were fascinating, and the class was a very welcoming environment. I would highly recommend this class to any student interested in children's rights, education, or generally the future of our country and the world!
PSY 1612 is a wonderful class that encourages you to reflect on your personal upbringing and the upbringing of others. This is a class I would recommend anyone to take; it opens your eyes to the many things you may have taken for granted as a child, and, being at Harvard, it helps keep your mind conscious of community issues.

Selva is the best and cares very deeply about the welfare of her students. She makes the class–– she always is willing to answer your questions, take time to hear your thoughts, and follow–up over email/Zoom.

The readings are not cumbersome at all, and the reading quizzes are quite low stakes. The book club is a fun experience as well. What I would recomend to future students is to make sure you plan your time accordingly with the book club to minimize any last minute rushing. Furthermore, as with most classes in the psychology department, you will get out more depending on what you put in. Thus, try to. take the time to sit down and digest the readings, and try your best to share your thoughts in class aloud. That way, you can extract the most possible benefit from a great class like this. As a graduating senior, at times I found my mind wandering about post–grad plans and thesis writing, but this class never seized to be a fun part of my day.

The class was also very enjoyable, as we had cool lectures and discussions. I enjoyed the book presentation the most. If you’re at all interested in child development, the role of social/systemic support networks, and/or parenting, then this class will be especially enjoyable and interesting for you!! I think everyone should take this class and can take something away that’s useful and applicable to everyday life.
