French 30 seriously leveled up my French. Before this course, my speaking was pretty bad, not gonna lie, but now I can actually hold conversations and talk about real stuff like current events. What really stood out was learning to understand and explain things in both French and English. It helped me see French media and culture in a whole new way. This class honestly changed how I think about language and made me want to keep taking French and maybe even pick up more languages while I'm at it.
