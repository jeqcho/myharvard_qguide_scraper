This class is great! Participate in the discussions and do the readings to get the most out of it. Start your final paper as early as possible!
I learned so, so much about US china relations. I think you should only take this class if you are ready to write the final research paper (as in you, have the experience in historical research and/or are ready to put in the work). This is not to deter you from taking the class, you should definitely take this excellent class, but you really only get out what you put in. The workload is generally not very high, so the onus is on you to put effort into the well–selected texts, engage in discussions, and workshop your paper. As an aside, prof Manela is an awesome teacher. I always looked forward to going to class.
This class is incredible!! Manela is a great professor who really cares about his students. Do the readings and start the research paper EARLY otherwise you will find yourself scrambling for your life towards the end of the semester. What you put in really is what you get out.
Excellent course, but frustrated at times with Prof. Manela's approach & needs some more guidance for the 'original research' component.
