I think this is probably one of the better expos classes to take. The subject is kind of niche so I would only take it if you like the materials on the syllabus. That being said, I did enjoy the materials on the syllabus. Expos is just annoying, but I do feel like this class made me a much stronger writer. Dr. Sweeney gives extremely useful feedback that will help you improve. He is really encouraging and wants you to do well and work hard.
Professor Sweeney is great, and I loved how he taught this class. He was always engaging and interesting. That said, this class is WAY too much work and stress for an Expos course. If you are really interested in the topic then take the course, but if you are just looking to do your expos requirement, take something else. My big frustration is that this class only focuses on how to write about literature and does not feel applicable to other fields.
