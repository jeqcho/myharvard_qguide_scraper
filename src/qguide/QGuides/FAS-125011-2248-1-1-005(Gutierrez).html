Ruy is an awesome teacher and is really supportive and flexible! Taking a 9AM section is rough but he really made it worth it! I feel so much more confident in Spanish now, having come in only remembering basic Spanish from sophmore year of high school (present and preterite conjugations & common words). He also grades very kindly, so it was great to feel comfortable enough to mess up so that I wasn't afraid to speak or write things incorrectly.
Ruy is the best section leader. His feedback is extremely detailed but encourages mistakes. Make sure to attend his office hours before every assessment because his feedback is very detailed and useful. The cultural, historical, and societal connections expanded my view of the world as well as my Spanish abilities. The class environment was very welcoming. Assessments can be difficult, but make sure to do the readings before class and be ready to feel dumb, and you will be fine.
If you get Ruy as your TF, your semester will be great. He truly cares about his students and improving his students' Spanish ability. There are a lot of readings for this class but they are generally pretty short and some of them can be interesting. From what I've heard, grading is very TF dependent. Some TF's are very lenient while others are much more strict.
Don't take the class in the morning as the teacher only talks in Spanish so you need to be fully awake
I would say be prepared to speak a lot of Spanish.
