Great class that teaches you about lots of real–world issues and solutions in tech policy without generating too much stress.
Take this course! Professor Sweeney is such an engaging speaker and has such great knowledge and experience on the topics we discuss. Our unit projects were a great way to explore the topics deeper, but also allowing us to tie in our own interests. The support for our final projects was great, and I loved the final project fair where we got to see what other peers had found for their research projects.
This is a class with really fantastic professors who have a lot of really interesting expertise to share. The quality class itself is, I think, up to debate, depending on the kind of student you are. Most classes were hands–on, which was very interesting and engaging. However, you didn't really get to hear from the professors, who personally, I was kind of there for. It's really not a lot of work, and you're not held up to a very high standard. That is great if you're, let's say, a freshman writing papers at this level for the first time––you don't deserve harsher grading! But, if you're a senior writing a thesis, maybe you don't want to take a class with an assignment called "my first research paper," or something of the like.
