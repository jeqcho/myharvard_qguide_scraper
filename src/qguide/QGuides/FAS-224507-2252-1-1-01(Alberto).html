What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This is an excellent history seminar to take if you're interested in cultural history approaches, Argentina, nation–building, or just the various interactions of gender, race, indigeneity, class, and more. Professor is super knowledgeable and kind, and really spurs discussion on the topic. It really makes you think more about various important processes in the pursuit of nation–building. Highly recommend. Readings are nicely seleced and paint an excellent, complicating picture about the various social forces and processes that went through Argentina between its colonial period and the modern day. The readings are long, and there are also weekly response papers, but come on, this is a history class.
Fantastic class! I'm not a history concentrator, but I had an absolute blast and had no trouble with the writing. Prof. Alberto is incredibly engaging, and this was honestly one of my favourite Harvard classes.
