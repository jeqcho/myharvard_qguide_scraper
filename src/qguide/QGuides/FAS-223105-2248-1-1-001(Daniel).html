What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
The topic and content seem intuitive at times, but that don't let that make you miss out on the skills you can take away. Take this class to do a deep dive on something you find interesting, and perhaps come to some original thought. Dr. Caitlin really helps you, is really approachable, is organized, prioritizes student interests, and tells you what you did well and how to improve with assignments. You get the opportunity to see what other students are working on and get support and collaborate. People like to think sociology is just 'reading' and not a robust or intellectually challenging field, but if anything this class can show you how we take a lot of concepts for granted, and getting closer to Truth may not be convenient or easy but can be really rewarding.
PLEASE TAKE CLASSES WITH DR. DANIEL. I was genuinely starting to hate sociology and feeling very lost in the concentration, but Dr. Daniel restored my faith. She works so hard for her students, listens, and wants us to create work that we are proud of. I chose this class because of the timing, but I'm leaving Dr. Daniel's biggest fan and actually understanding so much more about the world around me. You are allowed to disagree with her, you are allowed to mess up, and you will actually produce a piece of writing that shows your brilliance. Even if it's not this class, I cannot speak more highly about Dr. Daniel as a person, instructor, and educator. She has the magic touch!
This is another course in which you’ll get out what you put in. Although I wasn’t originally super happy about being put in a qualitative junior tutorial about health, I ended up really liking the material and the way the content was taught. I would take this course again if given the chance. Dr. Daniel is an excellent professor and gives great feedback. Please schedule meetings with her and attend office hours!!
