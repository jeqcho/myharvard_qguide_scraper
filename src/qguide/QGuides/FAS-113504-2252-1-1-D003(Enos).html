Political geography is a fascinating subject because it is so broad ––– anything that involves space. However, this broadness was somewhat overwhelming and if you don't read the research papers in this class carefully it would be easy to come away without having learned anything.
Choose a seminar topic that genuinely interests you even if you dont know much about it yet. Try to engage deeply with the readings and take note of structural and stylistic tendencies that you could mirror in your own prospectus. It's also a good idea to pay attention to which areas of the readings spark your interest most, as this can help shape your research focus for the prospectus and potentially even a thesis down the line.
10/10 would recommend.
Nice class but feels like an expos in the sense that you should really pick the topic that interests you the most. Noah is really knowledgeable about the content and your writing abilities in government will improve considerably. Make sure to do the readings and try to keep good notes so they can later be of great use when you write your prospectus

Overall very satisfied with this course, the readings, and course instructor
