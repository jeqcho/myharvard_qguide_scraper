What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
this class is half grad students and half undergrads and I loved it a lot! I had a lot of qualms about english classes at Harvard where discussions are cursory and not focused on close–reading. The first half of the class is spent on close–reading short stories where I really felt like everyone was engaged (the no screens policy helped as well) and I loved reading everyone else's stories and having time to workshop my own. I always wanted to find time to write but haven't had the chance in college and this was a super fun way to really produce works that I always wanted to write
