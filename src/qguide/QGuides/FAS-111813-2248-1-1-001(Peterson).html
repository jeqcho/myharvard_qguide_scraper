I took this class with chem 17 and neuro 80 and had no problem with it. The workload is like this:
– Video lectures (most important), Essay–style readings, and a textbook
– Quiz every class (Watch the video lectures at the very least and take notes! Review them just before lecture and you'll do well)
– Midterm paper
– Midterm paper presentation
– Midterm exam
– Final exam

The midterm paper happened to fall right around the time of my chem 17 midterm so make sure to get ahead on it.
The exams are fair and if you study, you'll do well! Focus on studying arguments for and against topics (ex: pros and cons of electoral college, strengths and weaknesses of the Constitution, anything with a pro/con argument in lecture or section) because they will be the focus of writing questions on the exams.

Overall, I recommend this class if you're interested in American government and politics!
