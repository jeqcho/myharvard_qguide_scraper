I loved this class!! I was skeptical about taking a 10 hour a week course, but I ended up enjoying it very much. There isn't much work outside of class (you get a lot done in–class) and the teaching staff is awesome. The experiments are fun and you learn a lot of new laboratory techniques.
Great class, one of the better chem labs!
If you like your orgo labs you will enjoy this class. We explored chemistry inspired by Harvard professors which was pretty cool. A few of them even came in to give us a presentation about their work. The class is very reasonable, as long as you come in ready to work, there's not much work outside of class. It cultivates a nice community of chem–oriented students and there's lots of downtime to talk to each other and the TFs about anything and everything. They do a lot to support you, you will not be left behind!
