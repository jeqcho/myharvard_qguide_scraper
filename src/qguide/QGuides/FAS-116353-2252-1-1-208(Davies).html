Based on my discussions with others, I believe my particular section of Expos ("The Art and Science of Learning" with Tad Davies) was an especially good one. The topics discussed were both interesting and broad enough that we had a lot of latitude to go in various directions with our research essay. Since I'm interested in CS, I studied and wrote about the connections between biological and artificial learning, while other people chose topics in social science or other areas. I felt comfortable with my writing before the course and so didn't find it too difficult or time–consuming.
