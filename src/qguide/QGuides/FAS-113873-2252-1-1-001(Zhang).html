Take this class!!! The teachers are amazing, everyone tries their best and is supportive, and going to class genuinely was the highlight of my day this whole semester. I had some background in Chinese, so I didn't have to put in too much time outside of class (it will likely be a decent time commitment if you don't have any background) but I think the class is worthwhile for anyone to take. Also, having taken Chinese classes before, I can say with so much confidence that this class is the best place to learn Chinese.
TAKE THIS CLASS!!! The teachers are ALL SPECTACULAR!!! They are so fun, so understanding, and so so helpful in learning Chinese. There is no other class and no other teachers I would have rather learned Chinese from, because they are that incredible. The class is definitely a bit of a time commitment, but it is so worth it, and this class has probably had the most payoff of any class I’ve taken at Harvard so far. Off–cycle (spring semester) is kinda nice because the smaller number of students makes a really personable class environment, but whether on– or off–cycle, the class is just an absolute joy and a great learning environment. Guo, Chen, and Zhang Laoshi are incredible teachers who understand so deeply well how teaching Chinese to English speakers work. They try so hard to make the class as good as it can be, and it absolutely works. There is no other language I would rather take here than Chinese because the department is incredible and BA is definitely in my top 5 favorite classes I’ve ever taken throughout my life! Also, I knew 0 Chinese coming in to the class, and I still got by really well! As long as you do the work, you absolutely don’t need to have taken Chinese previously to do well in this class. SO WHAT ARE YOU WAITING FOR??? TAKE BA!!!!
