This class is so enjoyable and rewarding. As long as you pay attention in class and submit all your assignments you will be fine. Professor Tragni cares about her students and what they learn. She is always available for help if something is not clear and encourages questions and outside help from class. Take this class for a language credit, but also a fun time where you are sure to learn a lot.
Professor Tragni is awesome – super kind and engaging. If you have any interest at all in Italian then take this class
Take this course, especially if you love Italy or Italian culture and the language! There are a lot of very passionate people in the Italian department, and they find lots of ways to not only ensure that you interact with Italian speakers at varying levels, but also that your own learning experience is personal and enjoyable. I loved taking this course with Professoressa Tragni and my classmates, and we all bonded over a semester and left not only with great bonds but also a greater love and knowledge of Italian. Yes, it is intensive and you have to put in the work and effort to succeed to do well, but this can be said of any class. If you're even a little bit passionate or curious about the Italian language, take this course, whether for the language requirement or fun!
Make sure to keep up with the VHL because those grades do count. Flash cards are key. Many resources to reach out to if you need. Take the 9 am class–– the community developed is awesome.
This class is an adventure where you not only learn a new language but also about culture and life in Italy.
I took this class and had little exposure to Italian prior (I am not Italian myself), and while it was intimidating at first, the professor really made the class worth it and as I kept continuing with the class it eventually became my favorite class of the whole semester since I felt very comfortable with the pace that it was going at. The course definitely keeps you accountable in terms of pacing of knowledge over the semester, and it never feels too hard or too sudden. The professor is funny and reassuring and accommodating and that made the class even more worthwhile to come to. I miss classes a lot (I am chronically busy) but let me tell you, this class is definitely not one to miss!
For a language requirement or not, great class to take as Italian is beautiful and they teach it very well here.
