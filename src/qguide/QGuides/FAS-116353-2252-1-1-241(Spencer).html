THIS IS THE BEST EXPOS! I have heard bad things about nearly every other expos section, mostly that there was a copious amount of readings and they didn't even talk about writing in the class, but this class was perfect. We had a totally manageable amount of readings that allowed us to dive deeply into our analysis of each, and then apply this to how we were going to write our essays. We also spent lots of time in class discussing writing strategies, techniques, etc, which I think should be the whole point of Expos. Dr. Spencer was very nice and a fair grader, although he did have a tendency to ramble/get too philosophical in class. I also think I got lucky with fun and engaging people in my section, but I also think that discussing sci fi sources just leads to fun conversations anyways. I 100% recommend this expos!!
This is a fabulous expos section and I couldn’t recommend it enough. Professor Spencer is very kind and chooses some awesome sci–fi to read (maybe I just have a soft spot for Ursula K Leguin and Ted Chiang). The draft—>essay progression of the class felt very natural; response assignments we worked on prior to submitting first drafts provided helpful information and got us thinking about our essays before we started writing them so that, when the draft due date came along, we had already developed thesis statements and roadmaps for our papers.

The grading in the class is very fair and follows the clear statement in the syllabus; for individual essays, submission of a first draft that is imperfect but has stuff to work with is essential, but, if you can get that in, Professor Spencer provides thoughtful feedback that can help turn the draft into a very effective final piece.

I know that Expos has accumulated a certain reputation that I care not to get into, but I felt that this class was always interesting and enjoyable, even when the semester got busy. There was a certain ebb and flow to the structure of class meetings and in–class discussions that I really loved. If you love sci–fi, you belong here.
