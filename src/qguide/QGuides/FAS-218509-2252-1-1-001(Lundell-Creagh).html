This is a super fun course! Ryan is very chill and friendly, and the class size is small so there are lots of chances for interaction/participation, which made the learning better. He's really organized on Canvas, so all the slides, assignments, and readings are already organized into modules at the start of the semester (chances to get ahead). One fun thing is you get to take different personality tests throughout the semester and compare them. The reading assignments help to make reading dense papers easier, then you discuss them in class. Ryan is funny and shows fun videos during class as well to supplement the slides. It's not a difficult class, and the exam is open book online. So the course is really what you make of it. I recommend asking lots of questions and talking to Ryan after classes to learn more about the material. He definitely is well–versed in the field. Take this course!!
Great class!* The instructor is very passionate about the course and you'll leave with a solid understanding of personality psychology. Very manageable workload (around 2–3 personality tests per week , 4 reading analyses over the semester).
This class is great, it has a lot of overlap with other Psychology courses, so it is pretty cool to see how a lot of these theories of personality interact with other areas in psychology. Ryan is also an incredible professor and person overall. He has a very particular way of teaching that is really inviting and he is very very connected with the undergraduate experience. He is very flexible, accommodating, and really smart. I think the combination of the class and Ryan was what made it so enjoyable. He is incredible and the class was also amazing!
I loved this course! Ryan is an amazing lecturer who really cares that students are able to express their opinions and questions about the content
