I enthusiastically encourage you to take this class. One of the best classes I have ever taken!
Take this class if you are interested in climate change and health!! Professor Jones is such an intelligent lecturer and a useful resource in the health field. The readings can be a lot some weeks, but try to start early. The final being 60% of the grade was also scary, but you get a lot of feedback on some initial drafts. The quizzes are pretty easy as well if you do the readings. Try not to procrastinate and choose a topic you like to make writing 15–20 pages bearable. Also, remember that this is a history class so you will not just be discussing modern issues for the semester.
Please take this class if you are at all interested in health and/or climate change. Professor Jones is a truly kind and generous instructor who really cares about building a class that's as convenient for students as it is interesting. The readings can sometimes be long, but so long as you work through them each week by spreading them out, it is very manageable.
You should absolutely take this class!! It's one of the best classes I've taken at Harvard (and I'm a senior). Prof Jones is the best professor I have ever had by far. He puts so much effort into helping his students and getting to know them. During the writing workshop, he leaves detailed and helpful comments on every single submission –– I've never had a professor put so much effort into helping me. The way he facilitates class discussion is awesome. I've been in seminars that are just students making unthoughtful comments. But the way prof Jones goes back and forth between lecture and discussion somehow prevented that from ever happening. The readings are fascinating, and I learned many new things every week. If you are at all interested in climate and health, and even if you aren't and just want to take an interesting seminar, I can't recommend this class enough. I wish I could take it again! Prof Jones is a gem.
