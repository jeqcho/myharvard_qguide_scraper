Take this class for a fantastic learning experience!
This is Korean BB, if you are reading this it's likely because you took Korean BA or are planning to take Korean BA. When I took the sequence the most common student was a Korean–American who wanted to learn the language after having heard it at home growing up. This means they are usually not starting from scratch but rather have pretty good listening skills at the very least, but sometimes have good speaking and/or good familiarity with the vocabulary already. This is not a huge deal as the course itself is not tailored to these types but rather accessible for a total beginner so don't worry about it if you are a total beginner. I mention this to characterize the position from which I am writing the review, as a total beginner and as a non–Korean person.

The main thing I'll say about Korean BB is that the workload is quite a bit higher than that of Korean BA. I felt like I cruised through BA for the most part but from the first week of BB I (and others) felt the significant uptick in the pace and amount of stuff we were doing. The workload itself is very similar to that of BA and follows the same weekly patterns, but the volume of new content is higher which makes it tougher. That said, if you want to learn Korean this is the place to be and I think BB is the more important part of the first year sequence as we covered things that are closer to real world use. For example, this is the semester where we learned to ask if people want to do things like get dinner etc, so if you are on the fence about whether you should take BB after taking BA in pursuit of actually getting to a point where Korean is useful for you, definitely take it.
Take Korean!! Keep on taking Korean!! So much fun and there's a great party at the end of the semester. You get tight with your section and get to know people pretty well. A highlight of my semester

It can be a little difficult with the vocab quizzes, and sometimes it feels like they move fast, but it's doable!
Elementary Korean is amazing!! The course is such a thorough, foundational course for Korean language and you leave the class having learned SO much—seeing the progress you make in real time is insane to see. The teaching team's support, language parties, and community–building goals come together to form such a great language–learning community within the Korean dept and you get close to your classmates and teachers. It makes it so encouraging to keep learning, between the friendships formed with classmates and connections formed with teachers. They are so lovely and warm, and class is an incredibly supportive space for this reason.
