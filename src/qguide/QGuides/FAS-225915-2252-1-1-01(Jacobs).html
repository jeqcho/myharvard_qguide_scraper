Prof. Jacobs is a great person to take a class with. He creates such a communal classroom environment and is so thoughtful. His syllabus could use a bit of work though. I think in this class we spent to long on the build to the civil war and not enough on the war, reconstruction, or how it is remembered. Also, I wish we hadn’t had either of the guests. They were not very helpful for understanding the course content. Overall though, I would still recommend!
Take any course with Professor Jacobs!! It really feels like he's your friend. His goal is always to maximize your learning, and he's open to changing anything about the course if you can show that it would make everyone's experience better. Look somewhere else for in–depth discussion of the actual fighting / battles, but this course will teach you fundamentally why the war was fought. Spoiler alert, the answer isn't just "slavery."
I really enjoyed my time in this course. The content was really well designed and the flow of material felt natural. Rather than focusing on the details of various wars, I felt like I learned a lot about the history of the question, "what caused the civil war". I would encourage anyone remotely interested in the subject to take the class!
