Highly recommend this course! You'll learn a lot in just a few months, and honestly doesn't require that much time outside of class.
SPANSH 10 is a very doable introduction into Spanish. I did not have a single bit of Spanish knowledge coming into this class, and am now coming out of the semester being able to hold basic conversations and hold my ground in Spanish way more than I ever expected.
