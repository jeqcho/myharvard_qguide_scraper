It is the best FYS to take, the professor is amazing and the class itself is amazing.
TAKE THIS TAKE THIS TAKE THIS!!!!! Mariano is the sweetest soul in the universe, and his love for literature is truly admirable. Plus, you get to read a bunch of cool stories!!! Genuinely my favorite class of the semester.
Mariano is so kind and consistently shows his expertise in class. He gives insight into the pieces we read that makes them 10x as powerful. He does tend to talk past class by 5 minutes, and there isn't so much conversation amongst classmates, though we are able to get our questions on the literature answered by emailing him with before every class.
TAKE IT!!! Prof. Siskind has a passion for this subject that I had never seen before, and he will pass it on to you. Not only that, but he's also an extremely kind and understanding man, and genuinely wants the best for you (he teared up on the last class!). The readings are lovely and mostly very low commitment, so you get to read as fast or slow as you'd like. You can really invest any amount of energy into this class and you will still enjoy it, although because of how great the books are, I recommend you invest time into all of them. The discussions in class are great, and Prof. Siskind loves to share his knowledge on the subject with others. The final project is super fun to do too, and is the only thing that will require more time from you. The class opened my eyes to Latin American literature and helped me gain a new appreciation of its intricacies and close relationship with LATAM's history. You can also read the books in Spanish if you know the language, which is a huge plus.
