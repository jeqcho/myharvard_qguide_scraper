I would like to tell future students that it's an excellent change of pace that it most definitely worth taking. The format and different learning exercise is absolutely worth it.
This course is very discussion–based, which can be incredible for some but potentially boring for others. For me, the discussions became something I looked forward to for every one of this course's classes, and sometimes they would be guests (the people who wrote the papers we're reading for that class), which was so cool. The final project can be hard to make it very persuasive especially if that is something you are not used to, but there are resources for you to always reach out to staff members if you are feeling iffy which helped me a lot. Use the resources and make the most out of the learning reading material and you'll love this class.

Although Prof. Lewis is retiring, this class is much more than the sum of it's parts and I hope thatin the future someone half as amazing as Harry Lewis can revive this class because it has been truly a life changing experience for me.
