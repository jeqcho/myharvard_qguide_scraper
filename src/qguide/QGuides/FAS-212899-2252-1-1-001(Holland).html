What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This is such a great course! Prof. Holland is extremely knowledgeable and great at motivating discussions. We truly learned how to think about inequality in not so traditional ways and from different perspectives and contexts in Latin America. Highly recommend!
I encourage students to take this class. Be prepared to do a lot of reading and writing, but you will learn so much and. it is most definitely worth it!
This course is a great survey of inequality in Latin America and Professor Holland is extremely knowledgeable about the topic. Be sure to start your final paper early as it is not something you can complete three days before the due date.
I absolutely loved this course. Professor Holland is very accessible and is very helpful in guiding both the research process and understanding important concepts within the course. At times readings can be heavy, but if you have some interest in the topic they are really interesting and helpful in understanding many of the problems present in the Latin American context. The final paper felt daunting at first, but Professor Holland does a great job of breaking downs the process and even has individual meetings to help you through your process. I would recommend this course to anyone with any interest in international government and inequality.
