This class can be quite frustrating. Because it is taught by a visiting professor, you get a very interesting and exciting perspective, but the department frankly does a terrible job of preparing these instructors to teach a Harvard course. I never knew what my assignments were, what we would be doing in class, or how long I would have to provide material for my instructors. I was always confused and I really feel like a large portion of the class time was wasted. It often felt like we were having one–on–one meetings with the professor about each of our projects, but the entire class was also required to be there. Alice is really cool, she just is not prepared to run a course effectively.
