Hands down one of my favorite courses at Harvard, coming from a senior! Prof. Lewin–Bizan is one of the kindest, most accommodating, and passionate professors I have ever had. The topic is also not too difficult to engage with and very applicable to our current stage as college students and emerging adults. I was able to learn a lot about myself through this class, and also heard some wonderful perspectives from my classmates. The assignments are made up of weekly reading quizzes, 4 super short responses, a fun group presentation, one midterm essay, and one final essay. I randomly found the reading quizzes to be the most difficult part, but overall everything was super fair and not too overwhelming. Take take take!!
Professor Lewin–Bizan is incredibly knowledgeable which makes it an interesting class!
