What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This class helped me not only understand my writing better but also myself. It was very helpful and I recommend it.
Expos 20 Studio with Professor Jody is a truly engaging class that you want to attend. The readings in the first half of the semester were quite interesting, and I genuinely enjoyed participating in the discussions, as they were relevant to us, the students. Many of the response papers were useful for the final essay, so they are also worth paying attention to. Of course, Jody's feedback and help with writing were essential for me, and these feedbacks was very, very good. Detailed, with examples, and overall very useful. Jody is a master at leading discussions, and his sense of humour, combined with his expertise in many areas, made the class more than interesting. He is a very understanding and flexible professor who puts his students first. So even though the course itself may need some editing in places, Jody made it as engaging as possible.
Take the most out of this course in terms of understanding the material, reading the materials, engaging in class discussions.
