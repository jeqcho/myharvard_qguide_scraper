TAKE THIS COURSE. It is truly such a gift to hear Leilani talk about craft, and I felt like I learned so much through just reading short stories with her guidance and notes. Not the most feedback–oriented creative writing workshop (I'd say it was more focused on craft and generating a good number of stories than really polishing a couple), however, this was not necessarily a bad thing. If you'd like to come out of a workshop with three to four first drafts, then this is the workshop for you most other fiction workshops will probably be better if you're aiming for one to two polished stories, though.
