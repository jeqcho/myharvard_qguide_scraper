What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Elaine is very intense. You learn a lot about how to work with a boss who you might not agree with or work well with, which is a useful skill for going into industry. She provides a lot of feedback which is a great opportunity to become a better writer and researcher, but I felt that she also made some comments that were unprofessional, e.g. criticizing students' clothing, commenting on their appearance/weight. The TF, Taha, seemed to put very little effort into this class – lecture slides were not posted on time and he repeatedly showed up late or missed scheduled meetings with students.
Course is interesting and having class one day a week is an obvious plus, but the course is not easy.
It is an interesting course and I enjoyed learning about it, but it does require you to be prepared before class. You have to do the readings since it is a seminar with a more intimate setting. Be ready to get cold called every now and then, and make sure to participate a lot to get what you want out of the course.
