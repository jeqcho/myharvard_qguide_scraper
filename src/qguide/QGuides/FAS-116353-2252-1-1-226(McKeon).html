Keating is absolutely incredible. Your preceptor makes or breaks Expos, and you couldn't ask for better. He is engaging, easy to talk to, and knowledgeable. Though some assignments can feel like a drag, they tend to be the parts that are required by the writing department, not actually Keating's directives within the classroom. As someone who loves to play and watch sports the readings are fairly interesting.
More than A Game is a really fun section if you enjoy talking about sports. It is a lot more reading and watching of long documentaries than I expected, but still a mostly pleasant class overall.
Keating is a one–in–a–million instructor who brings enthusiasm each and every day. The material is not super light but it is interesting and the freedom on the last writing assignment makes it so that the work is more fun than arduous.
Take it if interested
Great class and learned a lot.
Keating's Expos has to be one of the most chill Expos available. His feedback and class structure makes it really interactive and lets students feel that they can really say anything in the class and have it considered by Keating with an open mind. Workload is steady – sometimes it does feel like a bore and busy work to do the readings, but I will say that the final essay was a deeply fulfilling project for me, and Keating's help with resources was much appreciated. I got to dive into a really random topic loosely related to activism, and Keating welcomed my ideas very vividly.

Expos is always a bore for most students, but I am very happy that I was placed into this particular section. Yes, I did feel like a confident writer before I came to Harvard, but this course really prepared me for writing at this institution. As long as you do the work, you'll get through Expos!

To cap off, I really enjoyed being in a classroom with Keating this semester. He definitely made what would've been a deeply boring class into at least something I was excited for each week.
INCREDIBLE course. Keating is such a great teacher fostering a great learning environment. He helps and guides you through every unit, improving your writing immensely if you put in the work. While the topic may seem easy and fun cuz it's about sports, there is a lot of heavy work in the first unit and a half, but again, it is all to your advantage since it helps you with your unit papers loads.
