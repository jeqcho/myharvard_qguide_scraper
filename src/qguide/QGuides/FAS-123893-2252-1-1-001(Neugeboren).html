Fun class.Not a must take but you do leave it having learnt a lot about Game Theory. It's a chill and insightful elective if you're curious about Game Theory!
Keep up with the material as it scales in difficulty quickly. P–Sets take a deceivingly long time.
This course provides an engaging historical perspective on strategic interactions, making it a good introduction to game theory. The class experiments were a highlight—they brought the concepts to life and encouraged active participation. While the course doesn’t dive deeply into current geopolitical issues or advanced mathematical models, it still offers valuable insights into foundational ideas. Students looking for a more applied or technical approach might find it lacking in those areas, but overall, it's a worthwhile class with some very enjoyable elements.
