such a good skill to practice for things beyond just theater, I really feel more confident in speech and performance!
Take this class if you get the chance!! You learn so much while also engaging in a fun and lovely environment. Professor Bailey is great and pushes you to really learn how to use your voice and apply it to different texts. You will leave the class not only a more confident speaker but also more confident in your ability to work through monologues and scenes in the future.
This is a really great class! I found it helpful to be taking this class in conjunction with another acting class because I found that I had another space to practice the techniques that I was learning here. Memorization is a solid component of this course, so I would greatly recommend being on top of your deadlines for your presentations as all of that memory work will be done outside of class. In general, not much class time is spent going over deadlines, so be sure to check on Canvas and be aware of when different journals and reflections are due!
I would tell future students to commit to the performances–––they are the most informative part of the course.
Please take this course! Don't be afraid if you don't have experience as this is a very welcoming environment. You will learn so many new techniques to help you with any aspect of speaking or performing! There is such a variety of things to explore within this course (movement, grounding, speaking on camera, heightened text, acting techiniques and so many more!) Erika and Jonathan are also amazing!
Ericka and John were wonderful! Being able to work on monologues and seeing how you progress even if you didn't have too much experience with performing.
Take this class! Put in the work and explore you will not only have fun but also grow and develop in myriad ways.
