Mala and Myriam were great instructor and teaching fellow! Both are very kind and willing to explain concepts and ideas and elaborate when needed. The class allowed for great discussion and in–depth understanding since the lectures reviewed everything that is read and group work helps reveal areas of confusion. I loved the module assignments that allowed for creative expression and a break from more common rigid assignments like papers. Pushing you to think outside the box about something relevant to class topics with a partner was a fun challenge.
Take the module projects seriously, they do require a decent amount of theory and elaboration! You get as much as you give from this course!
This class is definitely professor–dependent. Prof. Reddy was very kind and Myriam always had interesting section material. This class is just a requirement that needs to be done though.
