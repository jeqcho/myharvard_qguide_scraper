What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
All the lectures require you to watch a pre– lecture of usually 45 mins to 1 hour and answer a quiz prior to the day of lecture(were Mondays and Fridays). You will be required to understand the case before the tutorial(was on Wednesdays for us) and also submit questions the day before. There might be be more number of hours required than it seems at first glance.
It is a great class to strengthen experimental design and proposal writing skills.
Highly recommended for comp bio, biostat, and systems bio students to have a more grounded and well rounded understanding of clinical biology that might serve the backdrop of their research work.
