Work is also fairly fun and easy; it felt like reading for fun rather than for class. Take this class!!

A great class for people both familiar or new to Dylan.
This is a fantastic course –– even if you are not a huge Dylan fan (I was not before the class) if you have any interest in music history in the 20th century you will thoroughly enjoy this class! Professor Thomas is a great lecturer and always posed questions and ideas relevant to both back then and today.
I would tell future students that this class is a great option for low–stress but fun learning if interested in anything music, history, or Bob Dylan related.
