What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
I had a lot of fun in this class. Freed goes really fast, so be prepared for something tough – but he also makes the content SO interesting and is great at communicating it. The final paper was really fun too, very exploration–minded. My regret is that I was really too busy to devote much time to the course. Grade–wise, it's not a problem; he's super chill and basically says at the start of the course that you should do the psets to the extent you can and you don't have to finish them. But if you want to really learn from it, make sure you have a solid number of hours each week to devote to it.
I strongly recommend this course with Professor Freed. His lectures are some of the best I've ever had in a math class. His organization and presentation of the content are unique. The workload is very manageable (weekly problem sets, plus a final paper). Both the lectures and the problem sets are insightful and full of useful examples.
