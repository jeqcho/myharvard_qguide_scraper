What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This is a great class! It gets difficult in the second half of the semester because the amount of content per week ramps up significantly, but Ivy is an amazing educator and it is so much fun knowing even a little bit of Latin.
It's straightforward, structured in a good way and you will feel like you learn a lot. Also now I understand cool Latin proverbs/sayings.
If you're interested in learning Latin for the first time, this is a great introduction. The course materials are amazing and there are a ton of examples and ways to practice. It's a lot of rote memorization, but if you put in the time, you will get it.
You'll be challenged, but will walk away confident in your ability to actually read Latin.
I would recommend the course for anyone wanting to fulfill the language requirement as I do think its one of the better intro language courses, however it is pretty fast–paced so dont fall behind.
Stay well on top of assignments and chapters, or you will fall irreparably behind. Utilize office hours.
