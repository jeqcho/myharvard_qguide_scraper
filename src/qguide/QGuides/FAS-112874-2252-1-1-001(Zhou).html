Professor Zhou is a great lecturer with much enthusiasm for the topic. Each lecture was super comprehensive and the sections/psets that accompanied them were very relevant.
SOCIOL 2203 is a great extension of SOCIOL 2202. I especially appreciated the opportunity to explore a wider range of models and apply them to real–world data.
I recommend this course! I learned a lot of different statistical methods including, poisson regression, ordered logistic regression, multinomial logit models, bootstrapping, a general overview on causal inference, inverse probability weighting, and difference–in–differences. In this course, you will complete problem sets in RStudio to better solidify your knowledge of the concepts. The course readings can be challenging, but the material is important and Xiang's lectures, lecture slides, and sample code are helpful. If you are hoping to increase your statistical skills, I definitely recommend taking this class!
If you liked soc 2202, you'll also like this class! Xiang is an amazing professor, and if you're an AM/Soc concentrator, 100% take this. Section is optional but helps a lot
