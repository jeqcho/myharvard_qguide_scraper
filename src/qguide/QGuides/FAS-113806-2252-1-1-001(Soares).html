Marcia is a fantastic teacher. Her passion for the class is contagious, and she cares very deeply about her students. I learned so much from Marcia and had fun at the same time. The course is very exciting and accessible. It is not very difficult, and the instruction is very effective. You will be blown away at your new skills when the semester ends. Also, I am not a native Spanish speaker and had no problem at all. Cannot recommend this course enough.
Portug 10S was my favorite course I took this semester! Please take it with Marcia – she is the best Portuguese instructor and really makes the class feel like a community! After just 1 semester of Portuguese, I feel comfortable holding basic conversations and having short interactions with Portuguese speakers in Boston – whether it's ordering food at a restaurant or just running into a Brazilian on the street.

There's not much work to do outside of class besides the occasional homeworks, compositions, or studying for the tests. I would recommend trying to create vocab sheets as you go so it's easier to study. Also, would highly recommend signing up for the optional Teletandem as that significantly improved my conversational Pt. and exposed me more to Brazilian slang and culture. The class sizes are small, which is great because you will become friends with everyone and perhaps even go as a class to a Brazilian restaurant in the Cambridge area.

One hidden gem is the weekly tutoring sessions that Marcia and the Portuguese department offer. Its 1–1 session where you can ask and learn about anything you want to. Marcia was so helpful in answering ALL of my grammar and vocab questions and would extend my sessions by 10+ minutes very often. It was so helpful to reinforce what I learned in class and accelerate my Portuguese learning. Please take advantage of all the resources this class and department offer!
