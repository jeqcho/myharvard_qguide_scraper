Very good course, much better than Spanish 20. Emphasis on speaking, and you get much better if you apply yourself. Alejandro is an amazing TF. Readings and songs are also pretty good, but make sure not to get caught up in understanding every word or it'll take you forever to get through them.
If you're placed into Spanish 30, definitely take it. I placed into 30 as an incoming freshman and didn't take it until second–semester sophomore year so I was nervous (especially since this was my first Spanish class at Harvard), but I'm so glad I took it. It's definitely a lot of work (homework nightly, reading, vocab/grammar review, and class three times a week), but class was always engaging and fun, and if you're serious about getting better at Spanish, definitely take this class. Alejandro was an amazing teacher and stickler for grammar (which is better for you in the long run), and always made class super fun. This class forces you to speak in Spanish all the time, so you'll definitely improve your speaking skills.
Take this class!! I really love the spanish department here and this class is a wonderful example of it. Also, utilzie office hours, the Spanish Program's Chat GPT prompts, and tutoring center!
