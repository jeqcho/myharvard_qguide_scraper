I would take it again if I could.
I would say take this class if you want a chance to learn hands on engineering skills. The TFs are great and will be your friends in getting assignments done. Additionally, I recommend taking this class when you are an underclassman because you get acess to the Fabrication lab for the rest of your time at harvard.
PS70 is an amazing class and probably the best I've taken during my time at Harvard so far. The teaching staff are great and you learn ALOT. The workload is generally very manageable and I rarely felt stressed about the class because of the accommodating staff. Also start your final project early and come into the class with general ideas of what you want to make.
As an engineer, TAKE THIS CLASS! Even though it only counts as an elective (which it totally should not), this class actually teaches you the fundamental technical skills that many stem classes at Harvard fail to do. It's a fun crash course on varying fabrication methods that you learn through doing. That said, dont underestimate the classit will demand a significant amount of your time, even though it might not feel that way because youll likely be enjoying every second of it. For this reason, I recommend lightening up your course work as much as possible if you intend on taking this class. Trust me, you will thank yourself later :) I don't know anyone that took this class that didn't love it
If you are a creative person but would like to apply that creativity to more technological fields I highly recommend taking this course. Truly this was one of my favorite courses at Harvard and you get to keep swipe access to the Lab which is such a perk!!!
