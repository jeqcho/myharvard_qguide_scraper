The textbook is top–notch, and the course brings you through foundational material including discrete and continuous Markov chains, MCMC, branching processes, Poisson processes, Brownian motion, martingales, and Ito calculus. The psets were not just theory but also included computational problems (with freedom of choice for any programming language), which helped make the material more concrete.
