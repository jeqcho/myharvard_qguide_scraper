I learned a lot from this course, and I definitely recommend it. Chris is a phenomenal professor who clearly cares about his students and goes above and beyond to support them. Be prepared to read and write reflections on your reading on core classical social theorists. Class is very engaging and helpful to solidify core concepts.
Social theory is foundational to your career as a sociologist. It's sensible to treat the course assignments as opportunities to evaluate your own research ideas in new light, illuminated by social theory. With particular emphasis on the final paper, aligning it with your research goals is a constructive way to fulfill the assignment while making progress in your research.
Christopher Muller is an excellent scholar and exceptionally thoughtful, careful, and sharp in class.
