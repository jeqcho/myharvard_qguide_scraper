What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Do not question whether you are interested in french contemporary literature or not this class will make you a better reader and critic regardless of which literary period interest you.
As a student from the Design school – so, non–traditional intersection with this course – I would encourage students with similarly non–traditional backgrounds to take this course, if they can.
Take this class (or any class taught by Prof. Kim)! She does an incredible job of leading discussion, always knowing exactly when to jump in to push conversation forward or blow your mind with a different analysis. The books are carefully selected and organized to build themes and understandings that you won't be aware of until the very end. They're certainly all worth the read, and this class will only be worth it for you if you're willing to thoroughly read them and engage. Being comfortable in conversational French is necessary, but if you're worried that you're on the cusp of being ready — just take it! Your french will improve a lot, as will your understanding of the francophone literature scene today.
This is an amazing course with an amazing instructor. Professor Kim is such a dedicated lecturer, and so attentive to her students. It is apparent that she is an expert in the field and shows her wonderful enthusiasm every day. The books through this class are phenomenal, and I truly felt that at the end of this, my French improved to significant levels, enabling me to hold conversations in regards to anything, and particularly, about modern french novel.
Professor Kim is one of the most remarkable professors I've ever had. Her ability to make students feel included and validated in discussion was unlike anything I had experienced at Harvard, and it's something I can not convey my appreciation for enough. The readings in the course are challenging yet totally compelling and really push your understanding of French literature, and Prof Kim's ability to weave together comments made our discussions so rewarding and insightful. I would totally recommend this course to anyone interested in improving their French at a higher level and engaging in a unique literary canon, if not for the subject matter, then for Prof Kim herself.
