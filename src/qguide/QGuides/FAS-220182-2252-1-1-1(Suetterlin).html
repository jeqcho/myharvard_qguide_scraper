Comments
This course is very accessible to students who have no background in German thought. It is a true survey course, with fascinating readings. You get out what you put in!!
it will give you a good overview of German history and literature.
Very chill way to get to study German literature, history and thought systematically! It was extremely helpful to read everything with a historical understanding and to see how thoughts evolved through time. The course materials were interesting and encompassed lots of different media. And the essays are not too taxing. If there's anything, I think I would prefer to have more of a discussion in class, as well as perhaps one or two longer papers to explore my interest as opposed to three 1500 words essay, which is a bit short to actually delve into anything.
There were many good elements to German 100 –– the texts we read in class were fascinating, and the guest lecture (we had one on Mozart) was excellent. However, the grading was extremely delayed, and sometimes lecture could drag on without much engagement from students.
