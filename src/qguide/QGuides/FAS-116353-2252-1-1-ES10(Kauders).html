I recommend this over regular Expos. Super helpful and provided a lot of insight.
This class was totally reasonable and a good class. Pace is slow and steady – you really work through the modules piece by piece. This means that the class DOES NOT provide unnecessary stress – huge points for that. You only need to write 2 papers over the course, and they are both super chill and you build the skills up and write them over time as opposed to simply being told to make a paper appear from nothing in a few days. I'm English and had decent writing experience before this class in high school, and I genuinely vouch for it as a great option instead of normal Expos 20. Much more laid back, and you get out precisely what you put in. I learnt a TON about myself as a writer through this more methodic approach to writing, and in the end I feel as though this knowledge has made me a far more reflective and competent writer in return. Every single person SHOULD CONSIDER EXPOS STUDIO. It is a GREAT choice for everyone. Very chill, very interesting. Hannah Kauders IS AN ABSOLUTELY INCREDIBLE PROFESSOR. She is the SWEETEST human I've ever met and she is so caring and helpful and attentive to her students and she will make you feel so at ease throughout the entire class AND WILL MAKE IT FUN. She's hilarious and class was genuinely amazing vibes WHILE we were learning and working hard. Nothing else you could possibly ask for in a class.
