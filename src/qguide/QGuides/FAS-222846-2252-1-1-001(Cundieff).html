What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
I recommend! Especially if you haven't really done this type of writing before I learned a lot
Past Selves and Future Ghosts is an amazing class and Melissa is a fabulous instructor! What you get out of this course is what you put in, so turn in your pieces and feedback on time! The feedback structure is interesting in that we don't give line edits back to each person that we're workshopping. I recommend typing out comments that you get back from classmates because they come pretty rapid fire. You'll get some feedback from a feedback cohort but not everyone in the class. You'll get feedback from Melissa as well, at your request primarily, after the first piece. You have the opportunity to learn a lot about yourself and your writing through this course, if you let it happen!
Take this class! Or any creative writing course with Melissa.
It's sometimes difficult to feel like you can write organically when there are a lot of high–stakes and pressure. This class creates a relaxed environment to freely work on your writing as you wish. To be noted is that it is a memoir–heavy class, so if you're trying to improve other types of writing (academic writing, very reflection–heavy writing, etc.), then this maybe is not the class for that. However, it is the class to help you own your voice in your writing and become better at writing anecdotes. Melissa also has been incredibly aware and understanding of the other things going on throughout the semester, so she is receptive to student concerns, which makes it easier for students to fully commit to the class, as opposed to rush through the material as if it is busy work.
