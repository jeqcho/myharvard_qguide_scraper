This is the best Expos. Take this Expos!! I heard so many people complaining about their Expos and I didn't even realize this class was supposed to be dreadful for people, because I enjoyed taking it so much. This class is fascinating especially if you're interested in privacy and any sort of Internet politics, or any sort of legal writing. We had some awesome discussions about things that felt very relevant to our lives as students but also as teenagers, and Sinnott was very knowledgeable and clearly very interested in the subject.
If you are interested in free speech, take this class! The conversations and topics are very engaging and applicable to current issues. In my opinion, Prof. Sinnott grades fairly and gives great feedback in the conferences. There are high expectations for clear argumentation in this course, but it definitely helped improve my argumentation skills. Overall, I found this to be a great Expos section...just do the readings!
Dr. Sinnott is really great, and the course covers a lot of interesting topics you may not find in other Expos courses. Try to adhere closely to the comments and feedback given and to submit things in a timely manner to receive said feedback. Meet in office hours and conferences as much as you can!
A lot of work, but it's worth it!
Dr. Sinnott is a great and caring preceptor. She's brilliant and has a lot of insight about free speech, philosophy, and law that are relevant, interesting, and helpful. Take this Expos.
TAKE THIS CLASS!! It is not too hard, the teacher is the SWEETEST woman you will ever meet who is very open minded and kind, you get two 24 hour extensions on essays if you want, it is okay to show up slightly late, the content is super interesting, and there isn't too much of it. My friends from the class and I are some of the only people I know who actually enjoyed their expos classes!!
It's absolutely worth putting your effort into improving your writing through this course. Take feedback as an opportunity to reexamine what you thought about your own writing, not as a referendum on your ability as a writer.
Dr. Gillian Sinnott is both an amazing person and instructor. Her feedback is very helpful, and the course is very interesting. The discussions engaged in this class were mind openers for me; my peers in this class had brought up their perspective on the same issue and came up with completely different proposals from mine, which were perfectly defensible and reasonable.
