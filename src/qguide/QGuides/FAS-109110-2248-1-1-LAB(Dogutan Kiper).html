Take this class! Dilek is great. She is super supportive inside and outside of the lab, always answering our questions and offering to meet outside of class. This class may seem daunting because it's 10 hours a week but you'll have a great time in the lab and the work outside of the lab is minimal.
This class is run very well, and the workload is reasonable. Even though it is a ten–hour a week class commitment, the post lab assignments are pretty quick and very reasonable. The first part of each class is a sort meeting covering experiment results or a short lecture about characterization techniques that will be used. There are also short excursions to see the crystallography lab or do measurements at the Nocera lab, which were both fun. Overall recommend (and you'll learn a lot of inorganic synthesis techniques!), and based on what others say about the other advanced lab courses, this may be the best and most well–run.
Take this class! Dilek is incredible in every way, and this has been one of my favorite courses at Harvard.
Dilek and the TFs were fantastic and there was a great community in the class. Take the time to understand the science behind what youre doing
Dilek is incredible and this class is very much worth the time. I learned a lot about lab work and manuscript writing and figure–making, and I had a lot of fun doing it. The teaching staff were all very kind and on–top of it, and they were always ready to help.
