This is an AMAZING course!! You really only have to show up and listen, and it's all super interesting material...especially if you're an athlete or a big sports fan. Definitely recommend for anyone looking for a gem.
This is a super chill, and yet super informative, class. If you have an interest in sports, or are trying to find a stress free entry into sociology, this is a great option. The readings are super manageable from week to week, and Isabel and the teaching staff are great. If you need any accommodations of any kind, they will make sure you have what you need and are super open to feedback if anything needs to change throughout the semester. We had one final 10–15 page group paper that we had to write in groups of four to five. Also, a midterm and final, but if you've been paying attention in lecture and doing the readings, you'll be more than set up for success.
Take this course! Professor Jijon is an excellent professor. Her lectures are so well–organized and engaging. She seems to be so passionate and knowledgeable. However, depending on your TF, you definitely need to put in the effort to get a good grade. The two midterms test you on large, sociological concepts but also on small details that only popped up on one presentation slide out of the possibly hundreds of slides that you see throughout the semester. But if you're interested in sports, even by a little, I would highly recommend this course.
