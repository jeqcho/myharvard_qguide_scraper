This is a great course when taught by Prof. Senger. His lecture style (+ Miller's notes) makes the logic of the course flow very smoothly, and you can really see how each idea leads to another. At the end I felt I had a very intuitive grasp of most of the material, and even if I did not fully understand something, I felt that I at least intuitively understood what the underlying principles were. Great class!
I'm not sure how hard it will be when Andy's not teaching it, but just make sure to spend time to just sit down and think about the ideas.
