Take this class!! Learning about such an important topic through a media lens is rare for an undergraduate course at Harvard, and I’d really urge anyone who is even remotely interested in any of these topics to take the course. The materials are really interesting and Professor Goodwin is a great instructor— you’ll never be bored in this class.
Truly liked this class. Sage was an amazing teacher and I really enjoyed all the different components of the class. Loved that the final paper was way earlier to focus on the digital media project, which I had so much fun doing
Interesting class and generally good assignments! The class covers compelling material and has a shorter paper (for a history class) alongside a creative media project. There are lot of guest speakers, which sometimes came at the expense of in–depth class discussion and engaging with the material. Planning ahead is key, especially around breaks, because the assignments are due around that time.
