Take this course not only because it is a gem but because it will change how you think about your daily life. Since I was very young, I have been deeply curious about the meaning of life, the best way to pursue happiness, what even defines happiness, etc. I enrolled in this course in hopes that it would give me a stronger sense of direction and alleviate the creeping feeling that perhaps existence doesn't matter at all. Indeed, this class is like a tasting flight of life–meanings for you to try on! The completion–graded weekly reflection pieces are a great way to apply concepts from lecture to your own life. The readings don't feel like work most of the time—they are of a very manageable length, and they are intrinsically enjoyable.
