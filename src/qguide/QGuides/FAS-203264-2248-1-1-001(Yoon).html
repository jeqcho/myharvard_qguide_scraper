What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
This class is great because it's an intro to writing that's very open to everyone. You don't have to study English or have written before to take this course. Paul is great and overall we have a great atmosphere and great conversations around the table. If you're wondering whether you might like writing fiction, definitely take this class. However, if you're looking for intensity and to write a substantial volume, this is not the best class for that. The workload is very light to the point where I felt like it was too light. We basically had to write feedback for the people who had written every week, as well as one story ourselves. We were also always let out quite early, and we skipped some weeks because of scheduling conflicts. In its current version, this class could definitely be taken as a fifth class. Overall, I think this writing class helped me to realize that I should take another writing class that is more work–intensive compared to this one.
To put it bluntly, you would be stupid not to apply for this workshop. Paul is an absolute delight and the workshop sessions always left me feeling happy and inspired. That being said, there is little to no work expected of you in this course, so do not expect to emerge a polished and masterful writer. It's great fun and a chance to work on one or two short stories.
As someone who had never taken an english class outside of expos, I would highly recommend this class. Paul creates such a welcoming and safe environment which is great because everyone shares their writing with the whole class. I would highly recommend to anyone considering, especially those who haven't taken a creative class at Harvard. All of the work is enjoyable, so it never felt like work!
Paul is wonderfully accommodating, but this also translates to himself haha! Know that he is very busy and occasionally may be a bit more focused on his own work than the class some weeks.
This is a phenomenal class. Paul is the sweetest, most understanding, kindest man, and he brings snacks!! The published short stories we read are so good, and since we read them at the beginning of the year, they're a great way to get into the short story narrative headspace in preparation for writing our own stuff. Reading my peers' work was so much fun, and it made me feel comfortable sharing my own work when the time came. This is an extremely chill class in terms of time commitment, but the reward is so high if you put effort in.
Take a creative writing class with Paul, no matter what it is! He's so cool and fun and genuinely cares for all his students. I learned so much about writing over the course of the semester, he's the best!
If you like creative writing, just take it!
