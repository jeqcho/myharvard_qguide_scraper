This course was really helpful in the thesis writing process, and Sachet was an incredible instructor! Each week, we either met 2x as a class or 1x individually with Sachet to discuss our thesis progress. There are multiple deliverables due throughout the fall semester that help you to stay on track. Sachet offered very helpful ideas and feedback in our one–on–one meetings, as well as in the deliverable feedback.
Great way of keeping me on track for my thesis. Obviously, it is still important to have a great, involved advisor –– I find that my advisor is really the person that keeps me on track the best. But this course forces you to hit certain milestones throughout the fall, which is helpful.
