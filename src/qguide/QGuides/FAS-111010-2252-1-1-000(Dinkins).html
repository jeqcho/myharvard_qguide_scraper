This course is a lot of work. You will be doing PSETs all the time, every week, and exams cannot be prepped for within a few daysyou should start at least 1–2 weeks early. Additionally, the course's content may be a little intellectually numbing at times, so it's important to find some source of motivation early on in the semester to help power through. Time management is also really important for this course, so try to alleviate your overall course load by not taking too many PSET courses at once.
