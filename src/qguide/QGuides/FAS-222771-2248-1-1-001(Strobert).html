Kianja and Will are absolutely amazing – you'll learn so much from them and I could not recommend taking anything with them more!
Kianja and Will are very supportive and willing to help you improve as an artist. You primarily get to work with oil paints, and you get to experiment with painting on different mediums. I would emphasize that this is a beginner's course, and most of the time in the course is spent on building the foundations of painting and learning about the process. The surrealist aspect of the course is more emphasized in practice at the very end of the course with your final project, but other than that, surrealism comes up more as a theme/topic of discussion rather than as a practice. That being said, you really get to work on the foundational aspects of painting that you'd like to focus on, and I would recommend taking the course! It's a lot of fun and you get to know/meet some of your classmates through their artwork and discussions too.
Kianja is the best! This course allowed me to learn about myself more and I would highly recommend taking this course!
This is a wonderful course in which you will build a strong foundation in painting under Kianja's wonderful guidance. She and Will are so helpful, supportive, and chill, and the vibe of the course stems from that. Expect to spend a decent amount of time doing beginner painting exercises. This course was incredible and made me realize how great AFVS courses can be!!!
