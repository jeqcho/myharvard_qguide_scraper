TAKE THIS COURSE! Professor Wang is absolutely incredible. He is so knowledgeable in the course materials and truly cares about his students so much. He always takes time out of his day for OH and provides very in–depth feedback which challenged me to get stronger at writing.
*Best balance between light workload and intellectual stimulation
Professor Wang selects and organizes the reading to provide a comprehensive overview of comparative political development across a diversity of regions. Every assignment in this class has a purpose and there is no busy work––there's 4 1–page assignments, a presentation on a reading, and participation. I will admit that it was quite intimidating to participate in class at times. The conversation moved very quickly and people in the class talk often, but it encouraged me to think more critically about the readings and think through my comments in advance. Professor Wang does a great job facilitating a welcoming class environment nonetheless. Note that A/A– is a common grade but hopefully evens out with the participation grade.
