What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
Take this class! The professor is amazing and very supportive of students who are interested in learning Igbo. The theatre presentations were super fun as our class got to prepare a short skit for other classes and professors in the African Languages Department. Exams were very reasonable and homework assignments were very manageable. If you are at all interested in getting more immersed in the Igbo culture and language, then there is more than enough room for Igbo AB to fit in your course schedule.
