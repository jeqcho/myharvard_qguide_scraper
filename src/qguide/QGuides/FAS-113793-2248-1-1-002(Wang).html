What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
The teachers are great and helpful. If you show up ready to participate and learn and review enough on your own you will succeed.
As a senior, my biggest academic regret is that I didn't start taking classes in the Chinese Language Program earlier! The teachers are all so incredibly kind, patient, and funny. You have to put in a consistent amount of work every day and every week but it pays off tenfold. You learn so much so quickly and have fun while doing it. Chinese classes have been some of my favorite courses ever and the teachers are some of my favorite people to learn from. They genuinely want to see you grow. I really cannot recommend it enough.
If you want to get much better at Mandarin, take this class! You will genuinely be shocked with how much better you will get at the language in a semester. The fact that the class meets five days a week is certainly difficult but I would recommend taking this class if you want to improve your mandarin.
This is a good class if you want to learn more about Chinese grammar structures and vocab. There are also some cultural portions that I found interesting.
Be prepared to be cold–called on, but don't be afraid to make mistakes. The teachers will help you answer the questions, and you will really push yourself to be better at speaking. Also, the structure is a little different because, on lecture days, you take a quiz on the words that you will formally learn during that class.
