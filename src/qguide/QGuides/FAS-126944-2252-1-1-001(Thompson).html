What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
I would only take this class if it is required. It tries to bridge the experimental and computational without quite effectively addressing either. Make sure you have a solid background in programming with R or python. The instructors are quite engaged and looking to improve the course, and it provides an overview of many genetic sequencing approaches, with a focus on the theory side, though assignments focus on application.
Probably a required course but not particularly useful. Lectures were technical but not applicable, homework required more coding experience than the prerequisites and office hours were not helpful, the packages used often had incompatibilities with Apple computers. The format of the journal club felt a little bit silly and the final project setup of people doing work then handing off makes it unnecessarily difficult to collaborate.
