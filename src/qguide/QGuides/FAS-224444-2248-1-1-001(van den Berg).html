Take this course! Lovely professors, lovely TFs, and classmates who are chill and interested in writing. This class was so relaxing and refreshing! Great environment and a great way to start the week!
TAKE THIS CLASS!!! If you are interested in writing with no experience or plenty of experience, this is the class for you. The readings for each class were so much fun to get through and it feels so good to look back and realize how many stories you've read in a single semester. That being said, if you fall behind on readings, it's easy to catch up and still make the most of this course. All of the assignments were creative and fun, so I'd recommend this class to anyone who needs a breather class if you're taking too many hard courses in one semester.

Great class that still teaches and practices writing skills without the application process and stress of seminars. A lot of covered topics and readings, some of which felt a little disjointed, but ultimately a very informative class with great professors.
This course was such a creative approach to teaching creative writing! I enjoyed the enthusiasm both professors brought to every class period. Their love for literature was palpable, infectious, and inspirational!
I would take this class again if it was a bit more transparent regarding the critical literary aspect of the course. I didn't love that there was an emphasis on that aspect, as I was expecting it to be mainly about creative fiction.
THE PROFESSORS ARE SO AMAZING AND PASSIONATE & the readings are interesting but the class is new and they're clearly still figuring out how to format it effectively–– lecture had little opportunity for engagement and section just rehashed lecture, with few chances to actually write or workshop. this class is so important bc it fills such a huge gap in the creative writing curriculum at harvard but i think to achieve its goals it needs some tweaking.
I am a premed student and i took this for my English req and, as someone who has been intimidated by English courses since high school, I found this course highly reasonable and not scary, it was rather very welcoming for me. I WOULD HIGHLY RECOMMEND this class for someone from a non–creative, stem background. I appreciated the balanced encouragement from the teaching staff to be creative while considering successful examples from class. This sense that I did not have to follow hard, strict rules to make a good short fiction story or a good essay made the class a significantly better experience for me than all my previous English courses. I loved my section TF William! He was a great facilitator who did a wonderful job with encouraging collaboration in section through group activity and games like Jeopardy! William also provided wonderful feedback on my ideas before the deadline, so I had the best chance of submitting work that would get me my desired grade of an A. So, I recommend you go to OH for your TF since they can be really helpful!
