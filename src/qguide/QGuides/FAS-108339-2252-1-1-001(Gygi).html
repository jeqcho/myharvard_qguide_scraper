Comments
This class offers a low–stress environment to learn about the fundamental unit of biology. Without too much time spent outside of class, I felt that I learned a good deal. The class–make–up has a lot of different expertise across DMS, and brought out interesting questions. The faculty were very helpful and accommodating, offering specific "expert hours" to help with the experimental analysis pieces of the problem sets.
I would tell future students that there is a lot to learn from this course while the course is still a relatively light workload. I really appreciated this during an otherwise very busy and hectic semester.
Valuable only if you are committed to learning material and willing to pay attention
