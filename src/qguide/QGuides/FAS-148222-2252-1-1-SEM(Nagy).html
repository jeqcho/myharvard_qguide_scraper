As long as THE Greg Nagy teaches, take his courses. He is a gem of a human and such an incredible professor. However, this class was really made even better by the incredible teaching crew as a whole. Each week, I felt as though I was in a fun coffee chat with people who loved what they were doing. I feel lucky to have taken this course.
This is a great class – Professor Nagy is fascinating, and being his student is a quintessential Harvard experience. The teaching team had many office hours, and made their own unique contributions to the class. There is a strong expectation that you will participate in class (and perhaps even be cold–called), which is not unexpected in a seminar, but can be awkward in a lecture hall. Also, please spend lots of time on your final project – there are check–ins throughout the semester to help with this.
You get out what you put in.
There was plenty of flexibility to pursue individual interests. The teaching staff was very understanding and made lots of time to meet with students.
Wonderful class! Professor Nagy and the teaching team as a whole are amazing. So happy that I took this class before graduating. Totally life changing!
Take this class! Greg is really funny and such a Harvard legend. The class is very relaxed and the content is genuinely interesting. You get to explore whatever you want for the final project, and the TFs are super helpful, so if you have a topic you're interested in it's a very rewarding experience. There's essentially no syllabus so the week–to–week discussion can be kind of random, and the people in the class can be really checked out (half of them are just doing other work on their laptops). But I had a good time overall, and I think you get out of this class what you put into it!
Take this course! The coursework is extremely light, and Professor Nagy/the TF's have lots of interesting things to say in class.
This class is so interesting! Greg is a great lecturer and I definitely recommend getting to know the teaching staff more one on one at open house. They're really genuinely there to help you learn and grow your passions.
This is such a great class to take!

What you take away from the course, and how rigorously you apply yourself, depends entirely on you.
