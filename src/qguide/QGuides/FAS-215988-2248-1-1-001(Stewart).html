Come ready to think outside the box and be crazy. Also if you're solely focused on the music aspect of things and don't want to think about characters or stories this probably isn't the class for you. But it will help you think outside of the normal and will push you
The class was experimental and therefore new at each step, but it was exciting as a result. I learned from my peers and felt more open throughout the course with the work I was producing. Though I didn’t have a background in writing scripts or writing songs, I was able to use what I did know and the knowledge from the class with the support of the professor, TF, and other students I was able to come up with a final product I was proud of.
This is an awesome opportunity to write a mini–musical on anything you can dream of! This course is specifically preparing the material to be used in the Spring Production Studio, so it was cool to write with the idea of students eventually playing these roles in a staged production. I would say there is a lot more independent work than group writing as we worked through our projects during this semester. Take this course if you want to learn how to write a cohesive, concise musical work from a teacher who values individuality, creativity, and nuance paving the way for untraditional musical forms.
