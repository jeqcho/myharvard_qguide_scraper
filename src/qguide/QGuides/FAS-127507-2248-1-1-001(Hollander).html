I think the point brought up in the last lecture that as important as this class is for PhD students to take, this kind of training would also be very important to new faculty who are learning to mentor and manage a research group likely for the first time. I think this could improve and address some of the issues brought up in the class such as power structures as well as rigorous scientific research.
