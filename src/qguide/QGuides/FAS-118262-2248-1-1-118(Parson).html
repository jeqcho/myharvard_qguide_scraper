This is a great introductory class for writing, but if you want to challenge yourself I would say to do go into Expos 20 because having to take two courses of writing takes away a class that you could have enjoyed to take. However, if you do not feel comfortable with your writing, then this is the class for you, you will receive a lot of feedback and the writing assignments will be broken down for you to be able to have enough time to understand what you need to do.
If you are not confident in your ability to write analytical essays that are more college level, take Expos 10. The course is relaxed, not too fast–paced, and helps you learn the aspects of writing you need for college. Professor Parson is an excellent and understanding teacher who knows how to approach everyone. His feedback is very helpful to improve yourself, and I think this is the best writing professor ever! He did a great job of teaching the class in a comfortable environment, making writing one of my favorite classes!
If you were recommended to take Expos 10 based on the writing placement result, definitely take this class. It's a slow–paced class that gives you the time and room to grow as a writer and improve your skills to make you ready for Expos 20.
everyone should take this class, specially with Parson. this was just overall a really good intro class to higher level writing at an easy pace. i was in between expos 10 and 20 when I first came in, and I'm really glad I chose 10
