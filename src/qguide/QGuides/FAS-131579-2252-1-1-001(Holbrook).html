The content is fascinating and there are field trips throughout the course that make it feel like a true community. Whether or not you are an IB concentrator, I highly recommend this course. Plants can be complicated, but with only 5 PSETs, one project and a final exam, it doesn't feel overwhelming.

This class made me love plants even more than I already did! I understand what the plants around me are doing now, which was my goal going into this class. Also, the creative project is awesome. Start it early and make something you're proud of.
