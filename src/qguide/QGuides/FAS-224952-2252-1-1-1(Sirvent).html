This course is by far one of the best I've taken in my college career as a senior, and a must–take if you're interested in any health profession. Roberto is a fantastic professor and invites a lot of guest speakers with lived experience in these fields to share their ideas and talk to you, which is so valuable. This is a space where you can be comfortable challenging things in your education that you have always felt icky about, and where you might even be pushed to challenge things you'd previously accepted as okay or normal. It's a long seminar, but it truly did not feel that way each time, and eeryone was always very engaged with discussions and the material. Truly no words can describe how valuable this course has been to my education.
I don't know if this class will be offered again, but if it is, take this class! Roberto is a great professor, and the readings are eye–opening. There's also guest speakers who come in and share their experiences around medicine as well, so you can connect with people who are involved in solving some of the issues with medicine brought up by the class.
