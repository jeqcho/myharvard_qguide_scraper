AMAZING CLASS! I absolutely loved the class and the dynamic with professor Izadi!
Professor Izadi is the BEST professor at Harvard. She’s so kind, funny, and supportive. You’d not regret taking a class with her. Take this class and all the other classes she’s offering. She’s truly an angel.
Even if you do not take this specific class, TAKE A CLASS WITH PROF IZADI!! She creates such a welcoming and vibrant community in her classrooms where you will get to learn, reflect on current events, and have fun!
TAKE THIS COURSE!!! If you have any interest in social justice, environmental justice, climate change, law/legal history, or just want to understand the indigenous struggle better, then this is the class for you! This class is a true nothing–to–lose class. the work load is super manageable, and the content is interesting. Professor izadi is super willing to work with you to deliver assignments that make the most sense for you (my final was a series of substack articles!) this course genuinely changed me – it covers one of the most oppressed and marginalized groups in the world, and transforms your understanding of US–Indigenous relations. It also teaches you to think deeply and critically about law, war, oppression, and so much more. I looked forward to the discussions every week, and meeting 1–1 with prof izadi.
I would tell future students that having an interest in indigenous ways of life, oil, and the environment makes this class a perfect fit for you.
