Persian is a great language and there aren’t many opportunities to learn it, so I recommend this class! Also, you can learn a lot of Persian grammar quickly so even completing elementary gets you in a good spot.
Persian is such a beautiful and rich language, you should definitely take this class if you are interested in learning more about it. This being said, this class is a good amount of work considering it is an introductory language, so be prepared to put the time and effort in that is needed to succeed!
Bezhan is incredible, picking up from a chaotic end of Persian AA, I feel like the amount of information and language skill I learned this semester is orders of magnitude higher than in my first semester. Classes are free–flowing but with a general structure of progressing in the textbook. The highly collaborative, small classroom environment makes for a perfectly enjoyable and productive class.
