I think I underestimated my math skill level when I decided to take MA and MB. It probably was helpful, I did not feel challenged most of the time.
Assuming that you have already taken Ma, Mb is the second course of the M series. Throughout this two–part course, I have contemplated many times whether I should follow through with the M series or to switch to another math course—and I am sure a few other peers of mine were on the same boat. I chose to stick to Mb in the end, and I do not regret as it has surely given me a boost of confidence in my math skills. I believe that Math Mb was very well–taught—just make sure to take advantage of the resources provided (that is, go to office hours)!
It is a great class!
