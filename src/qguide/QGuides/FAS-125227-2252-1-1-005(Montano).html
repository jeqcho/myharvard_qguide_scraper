Absolutely take this class! Could not recommend it more highly.
I think every single person at Harvard should take this course. Not only is it such a fun class but it is so necessary for everything in life!! Such an important skill to learn and such a fun way to learn it.
Take this class! I took EXPOS 40 senior spring and it has been my ~favorite class at Harvard.
To get the most out of it, you should be willing to "buy in" to the improv games and warm–ups –– this means being ok looking silly sometimes. It's worth it because by the end of the class, you'll feel very connected with the other students in your section (having heard each of them give many speeches about personal topics, done improv games with them, and given/received feedback from them, etc.).
Expos 40 is a fantastic course! There were many, MANY opportunities for feedback, the class environment was supportive with room for experimentation, and it was a fun way to practice a practical skill while getting to know a group of people you otherwise probably wouldn't. James is fantastic –– he's very knowledgeable, and really encourages us to lean into the at times silly nature of the class while making sure we always knew the concrete reason why we were were doing whatever we were doing.
