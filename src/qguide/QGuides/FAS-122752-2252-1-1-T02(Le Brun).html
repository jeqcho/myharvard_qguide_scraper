Comments
This is an excellent elective. The readings are all very interesting while still being relatively manageable, and Dr. Baranga is an incredibly dedicated instructor. He provides detailed feedback on all assignments and makes class very engaging while ensuring that the material is accessible and easy to understand. I liked how the course brought in the real world — this semester was one with a lot of trade policy news, and we got a chance to discuss current events in the context of what we had learned. In sum, I'd highly recommend this option for your sophomore tutorial.
Baranga is an accomplished researcher. Maybe not so much a teacher, but he does know a lot about what he's teaching. This guy would pull research papers from the 90s from his memory when asked about a random topic in econ, and I appreciated it. I do not know how he grades, however. Participation is a little weird and arbitrary, as well as the way he grades essays. Also, the tutorial was a little boring in class, as there would rarely be interventions and people would sleep in class.
Very interesting overview of the nuances of trade policy. Highly recommend, especially due to today's relevance. Assignments were extremely doable, and Professor Baranga is extremely helpful in providing feedback and advice
