What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
This class is great and extremely doable. Pay attention to the boxed information, space out your work on psets, and go to MQC!
This is a great introductory class to math, especially if you did not had advanced math classes in high school. I think the only thing to note about this class is that the workload can get unmanageable if you tend to leave stuff last minute because for most of the week you only have 2 days to finish each problem set. The TF's were really helpful in making the class way easier, this class has a lot of resources for you to be successful so make sure to use them.
Have a firm baseline in math knowledge before taking this class or have lots of free time to allocate to this class.
PLEASE take advantage of Office hours! MQC can get really busy, and I feel you learn a LOT or hone in certain concepts through Office hours that are not always explicit. I recommend literally doing your pset in Office Hours as you can collaborate with others and get your questions answered on the spot. The teaching staff is SO wholesome and supportive, so if you have any questions or concerns REACH OUT! Also word of encouragement: It is not over until it is! The first exam can always be really hard, but there is always the next exam, skill check or pset to improve on! You got this :))))
