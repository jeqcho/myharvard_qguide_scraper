you get from it what you put in
The teachers in this class are amazing! Co Hoa, Co Thu, and Thay Nhan, if you are fortunate enough to have them, are some of the kindest and sweetest teachers youll find on campus. They are all really passionate about 1) helping you learn Vietnamese, and 2) sharing the culture with you. Even if youre a heritage speaker, this class will be useful as youll learn new words!
120B was my favorite class this semester! The teachers are fantastic and there is a great community within the class. Highly recommend taking Vietnamese if you want to take a language class/get a citation at Harvard
Very great and manageable course! You'll be engaging in a lot of discussions, giving presentations, and writing in your homework. As long as you keep up with the new vocabulary, you should be good. It's a very comfortable and supportive learning environment, and the Vietnamese teaching department is absolutely amazing.
If you are debating whether or not to continue with taking Vietnamese, I would definitely recommend saying in the program. As someone who has taken classes in multiple language programs, the close–knit feel and relationship you make in VLP is unlike that of the other programs. There's an automatic acceptance into the community, regardless of what level of Vietnamese you come in with and whether or not you are of Vietnamese origin. The teachers care so deeply about making the program thrive an helping students; you couldn't ask for a better class to take.
