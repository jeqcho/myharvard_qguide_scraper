This class is a great class for your citation. You will definitely learn things about the world that aren't covered elsewhere. Some of the topics in class are hit or miss (it's impossible to be interested in everything) but the assignments are very reasonable and classes with Wilnomy are always great! The reading is very dense and difficult to understand towards the middle of the semester. Because no one understands the reading however, it mostly works out.
Amazing class! Not too time consuming and readings are super interesting.
Wilnomy is the BEST. She made the classroom such a welcoming space, and I was excited to go to class every day. I will say that in my opinion, this course was definitely more difficult and more work than Spanish 40 or 50, but overall, still very manageable.
Take this class! Keeping up with the readings will take a bit of time, but the resulting discussions are worth it!
I loved this class, but beware that the readings are not easy. Do the readings ahead of time so you aren't rushed and can pay close attention to them. Keep a running list of new vocabulary and grammar structures. There are no grammar lessons at this course level, so you have to take it upon yourself to brush up on grammar. If you don't, you will find it difficult to contribute meaningfully to course discussions. With that said, Wilnomy is an amazing instructor. She is very patient, understanding, and encouraging. Don't be scared of the readings, just take them seriously. There is a high potential to learn from this course.
