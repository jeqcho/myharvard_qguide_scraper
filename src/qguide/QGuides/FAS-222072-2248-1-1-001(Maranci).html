The ideas discusssed in this class can be applied to a variety of cultures, which I think is also interesting. To me, the class sometimes felt more like a history course than an art history course as I think it was more so studying history with an artistic lens as opposed to studying art with a historic lens. While those sound similar, there was a distinction to me that I did not expect. The grading can be a bit harsh or unpredictable at times, but I also think this course is very TF–dependent.
