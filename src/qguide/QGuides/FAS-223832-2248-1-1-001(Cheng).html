If you are even slightly interested in education, take this class. It is mostly a discussion style format where students discuss the readings from the past week as a class and grapple with both sides of an argument. You don’t need to know anything about eduction coming in, but you will leaving having learned so much. So many interesting topics like the purposes of education, who should be responsible for education, and more. The readings are also genuinely interesting and the professor and teaching staff are absolutely delightful. We have several speakers come in over the course of the semester and was it an excellent way to connect with people in the educational field. TAKE THIS CLASS!!
This course was wonderful. The course staff were very thorough and intentional in their feedback, and the people who take this class all offered unique and thought–provoking perspectives. Take this course if you want your view of education to be challenged!
This is one of the best courses I have taken! It's truly insightful and Professor Cheng is not only a teacher but someone who makes the class enjoyable. Because of this class, I have declared a secondary in Education Studies
I would highly recommend this class. The teacher and TFs are SUPER nice and understanding. There is only three big assignments (no exams) and they are all very flexible in format and subject matter. The class feedback is extensive, and the lectures are super chill as it is mainly just discussing the topics in the readings with your classmates.
This class is a lot of fun and a very approachable way to get some theoretical and academic backing to your own experience as a student. You'll learn a lot about a variety of important issues in education today. The course staff is extremely kind and helpful, and they make an effort to make the assignments not that intimidating and to offer a lot of guidance and feedback. Overall, this is a great course!
A super fun, engaging, and thoughtfully designed class! The discussions were so thought–provoking and enjoyable, and you could really tell how much thought & effort Liao and the rest of the teaching staff put into making this course the best experience possible.
This class is super interesting and a very reasonable workload. The readings aren’t super long so read all of them so you can participate in all of the activities. The professor and TFs are very accessible and really value student opinions!
This class is amazing. This class is one of my favorite courses I have taken and by far was the most 'transformative'. This course truly encourages you to think about education in a new way through wonderful guest speakers, class discussion, readings, and personal stories from classmates. This course challenges you to redefine your own beliefs about education and inspires you to think of ways to improve our educational system. It brings up interesting questions such as 'What is the goal of education in our lives, society, and world?' that are really fun to explore. The workload was very reasonable and the class was so engaging that everyone was always disappointed when the class was over. I cannot recommend this course enough!
