The course is really fun and the teachers are super welcoming.
The teaching staff is amazing!! Definitely a learning curve for me, but you get to know your classmates pretty well, and the course material is pretty engaging. You get a wide variety of students with different Chinese skill levels, with some being almost conversationally fluent to others who didn't speak much at home. The course material, in my opinion, was difficult, but the teachers don't want you to fail, so they grade pretty easily and there are bonus opportunities. You learn a lot in this class, but it moves so fast that often, it feels like the grammar portion doesn't truly "sink in."
Instructors are incredibly engaging! Du and Huang are full of energy and never fail to make language learning fun, interesting, and memorable. Class slides are helpful, with applicable grammar structures. In–class activities, such as role–play and various review games, are both good reinforcement and quite entertaining. Teachers call on students to answer various questions in Chinese, which can be nerve–wracking at first but is very helpful for practicing and learning. The class community is very supportive, and you will definitely make a lot of new friends and have good laughs.
