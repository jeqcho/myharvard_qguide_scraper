What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Table for [QTitle].
Comments
I would not recommend this course due to the excessive work and focus more on fusha rather than a balance between speaking and writing. The textbook was also not helpful and often made topics and concepts more difficult and complex than they needed to be.
Do not take this class unless you are willing to put in an incredible amount of time and lowkey have a bit of a background in Arabic because they move really fast.
Be prepared to work very hard. If you've made it to Arabic AB, you're familiar with Al–Kitaab. This class is purely focused on Lessons 5 through 10, so it is important to be comfortable with the textbook.
Perhaps the biggest piece of advice aside from that is to really try to review vocabulary and grammar from previous lessons to avoid confusion and/or falling behind.
This class is challenging but incredibly rewarding. Stay consistent with the vocabulary and grammar practice, participate actively in class, and you'll be amazed at how much you can learn in just one semester.
If you love Arabic, take this course and beyond. I won't lie, though, it definitely is a larger time commitment, so just keep that in mind.
