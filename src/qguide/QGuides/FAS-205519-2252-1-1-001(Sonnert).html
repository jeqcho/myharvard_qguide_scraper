Take this class! It's such a great experience, the class is lively and active, the lectures (the few there are) are creative, and it's a nice break from the more difficult classes in your schedule.
This class is amazing. The professor and course design are so engaging. Never a dull moment. Great class for social science students looking to engage with applied sciences.
I didn't expect to enjoy this course as much as I did. Like most, I heard that it was a low time commitment, and that was initially very attractive. However, even though it was a 9 am, and I took it as a senior, each class was so interactive, interesting, and prompted me to think differently about the world –– everything was so fun that it didn't feel like "just another 9 am course". The professor and TF were both amazing people who are open to everyone's thoughts on the matter. The students in the course came from all different academic backgrounds, so it truly felt refreshing. I cannot recommend this course enough :)
The uniqueness of this course is just incredible and has led for me to rank this course truly as one of my favorites in my college career. Professor Sonnert and Shelley are an amazing duo and lead with such compassion. I respect and admire how intentional Professor Sonnert and Shelley are when it comes to class materials as they want to ensure that students are able to form their own thoughts and opinions about concepts rather than just telling them what to think. The classroom activities are very fun as well! This is a very interactive course but enrollments are very limited to keep the space intimate, so if you're lucky enough to get a seat definitely take it!!

THIS CLASS IS A MUST TAKE! I don't even know where to start!! First of all, a class on space (sci–fi, UFOs, aliens, etc), are you kidding me, that was soooooo cool! Also, the class has so many fun/creative assignments like "write about a human behavior from the perspective of an alien" that was such a fun one. Also, the prof and TF are such sweet souls. You can tell just how passionate they are about the subject matter and there is nothing better than to be in that type of environment. Also, this class truly is stress free, its just one massive discussion for about the first 15–20 minutes of class, then the rest of the time is dedicated to a lecture component or a fun activity such as solving puzzles, a model UN, drawing aliens, etc. You will not regret this class so please petition for it!! Petition for it every year if you have to until you get in!
