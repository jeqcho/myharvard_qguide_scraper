This is a great gov class! Prof. Costa and Talei were a great team. The material is very digestible and there is a lot of in class discussion about current events. Good class to take regardless whether you prefer exams or papers.
If Mia Costa ever is visiting at Harvard again, please take this course. Prof. Costa was simply the best professor ever. She was incredibly enthusiastic and taught INCREDIBLY PRACTICAL CONTENT! There was so much to learn about congressional representation, and the content wasn't just theoretical –– the lectures (and oftentimes the readings) were based on field experiments and could be applied to situations today. She took ~10 minutes every class to ask students about current events. She learned everyone's names (which is big for a lecture!), and was just a really, really pleasant professor. This was a great class.
This class changed how I think about representation. I loved being able to really engage with research that I never knew, and it challenged me to rethink about many of my preconvcived understanding of American politics. Prof. Costa is just wonderful. The syllabus is so engaging. I could not recommend this course enough. 10/10
I do not think Professor Costa will be here to teach this course next year, but if she is, I 100% recommend this course. I learned so much about the inner workings of Congress and how it represents America.
I would say that this class has very interesting theoretical concepts about representation, but the data–driven analysis falls flat because it is insanely predictable. A friend and I both said things along the lines of "we could have come to this conclusion ourselves, the data is just something extra that proves we are right." This is not to the fault of Costa, I found her to be very nice and engaging with the class. However, it makes the class less interesting than others, so consider this if interest is a priority. The assignments were reasonable, and the grading was fair. Overall, solid class but not the most mind–blowing thing you will take.
Be eager to jump into discussions!

TAKE THIS COURSE! Prof. Costa is amazing and she always brings energy to the classroom. The readings, assignments, and overall structure of the class is great!
