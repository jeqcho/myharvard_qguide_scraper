TAKE THIS! such a timely topic and super engaging seminar discussions! loved the experience:)
This is hands–down one of the best Gov94 seminars that you can take, especially if you want to learn about AI through the sociolegal/governance lens.
Professor Liviatan is INCREDIBLE! She makes it easy for all students to participate, and everything she includes in class serves a purpose that becomes clearer near the end of the semester. The group & individual presentations can be quite heavy work, but as long as you put in effort and time, the class will be super engaging and worthwhile.
This course fundamentally challenged the way I understand technological innovation and specifically in AI. Taking this course enabled me to have so many great conversations about AI in and outside of the classroom this year, giving me a more solid grasp of what the biggest dilemmas facing AI innovation is in the modern age. The workload is quite manageable, and Prof. Liviatan makes the course so engaging! The second half is all group project research, which I felt like was a fun way to learn from my peers. There's no other class like this at Harvard. Especially as a Gov kid, it was so enriching to take this and learn about the intersection of tech and regulation.
