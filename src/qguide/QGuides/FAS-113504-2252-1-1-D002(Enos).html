I loved the in–class discussions about the readings, so make sure you read them, so you can participate. Also, going to office hours for the assignments were helpful!
This is a requirement, you don't have a choice to take it. That being said, it can be as challenging as you make it. Start your work early, strategically read, and engage with your TF –– you will be fine.
The GOV–97 tutorial will help you improve a significant amount of skills required for later on in your academic career!
I think it's important to find a course topic you enjoy for Gov 97; it can make a big difference in how interesting/enjoyable you find the class. Matthew is a highlight of this course! Really good at meeting and being supportive outside of class. The one thing about Gov 97 though is that so many people take it, so just try to stay on top of your grades since there's a limited number of As that can be given out in this class.
The content of this course was relatively interesting, and the TF was very knowledgable and willing to engage on content and support student efforts to improve. However the format of the class can feel like it drags at times, and it sometimes was not the most engaging course.
