Take a class with Durba Mitra! The classes I have taken with Professor Mitra, especially this class, have restructured how I approach academia and reframed how I read the world around me. Everything on the syllabus has added vital perspective to my understanding of systems of power.
You NEED to take this class. It has the best syllabus and structure I've ever had, with regular engagement with Harvard institutions like the Peabody Museum and Harvard Art Museum, as well as the authors of some of the papers we read. This was an incredible class and I learned so much about the various ways in which colonialism has shaped institutions here and around the world.
Take this class!!! Professor Mitra is the type of professor that only comes around once in a blue moon. Her status as a preeminent scholar in her field is evident in every single aspect of this class. Her ability to guide discussions borders on superhuman and the assignments are carefully designed to be engaging and constructive without feeling like busy work. I felt like every week in this class fundamentally changed me as a person (for the better).
Read all of the readings– they honestly help transform your perspective on certain topics
