Take this class!!! It is great and so interesting and you learn so much ASL so quickly. It also is fairly easy while being really effective which I always thing is so important for a class. Take Dr. Conlin if you can because she is amazing and so sweet and understanding.
This course with Dr. Conlin was FANTASTIC! I had never thought I'd be someone who would take more foreign language than required, but I'm heavily considering taking ASL 3 next semester! The course is well structured as a continuation of ASL 1 (though might be different depending on if you switch sections), with lots of time to review baked in. I found a noticeable improvement in my communication skills in understanding from the first to second semester, and even when there were times I was behind and admittedly embarrassed, Dr. Conlin was incredibly kind and understanding and helped me catch up in the course. She is also incredibly knowledgable! Daily class was engaging and I really found myself making friends. I cannot recommend this course and department enough!
I cannot recommend ASL enough, especially to fulfill your language citation. You will gain so much from this course! Also, the workload isn't too heavy or tedious. It will definitely require work, especially dpeending on which teacher you have, but all of the work is engaging. You will also have contless opportuntiies to immerse yourself in the greater Deaf communtiy which is a great experience.
TAKE ASL!! I've learn so much about Deaf culture that I otherwise would've never understood.
You should absolutely take this course! The professor is absolutely incredible; she's super dedicated and is just an incredibly nice, supportive person overall. It's not too much work, and the assessments / class overall are relatively low stress.
EVERYONE SHOULD TAKE ASL!!! You will learn so much in this class, while simultaneously never feeling overwhelmed with the amount work as the majority of learning happens in the classroom. If you are unfamiliar with Deaf culture or the Deaf community this class is extremely eye opening and will introduce you to an entire world you knew nothing about.
This is the best class I've ever taken. If you are thinking about it...DO IT. Conlin is so funny, so awesome, and a really great teacher. We learn not only ASL, but really interesting stuff about Deaf culture and Art. We play fun games essentially every class, and Conlin is a very flexible and kind teacher. I ended up learning way more than I thought I would, and I would consider myself very conversational at this point.
