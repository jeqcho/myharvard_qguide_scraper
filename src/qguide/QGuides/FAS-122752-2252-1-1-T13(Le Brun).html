What would you like to tell future students about this class? (Your response to this question may be published anonymously.)

Comments
Take the Melkonian Section!! Any sophomore tutorial you take is going to be a lot of work; this one definitely had its fair share, but Melkonian is phenomenal! His rubrics are very straightforward, so if you simply do your work with the rubric, you will have success. He is accessible outside of class and understanding of needs. Strict on little points on essays but all around not an incredibly harsh grader! He wants you to succeed!! If this is offered again, you should definitely take it.
Prepare to put in time and effort into getting the readings done before class as well as working to improve your writing throughout the semester. Be sure to go to office hours toward the end for help on the final paper as it is incredibly helpful when you are polishing your code for data analysis in addition to knowing what to include/how to interpret your data findings.
