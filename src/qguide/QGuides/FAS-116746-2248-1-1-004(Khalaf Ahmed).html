Comments
Take this class if you are serious about learning Arabic. You have to put in the work in order to succeed!!
This course dramatically improved my ability to read and write in Arabic and grew my love for the language. While it is a lot of labor, it is very rewarding. I would strongly recommend asking for office hours as much as possible and consulting faculty and peers for help.
If you’re willing to do a lot of homework all the time, it’s a fun class and you will learn a lot. It’s a healthy challenge, but a challenge nonetheless. Do the homework on time otherwise it builds up like crazy because there’s lots of different kinds of assignments.
Phenomenal introductory course. While it may look (and be) a tad time–consuming, the benefit to everything you do is well worth your time. You need to put in the work early on to get any sort of results with language, and AA is no exception. The teaching staff is incredible, as was this section of students. You won't receive a poor grade for trouble with the material, the emphasis is on learning recursively. That is, learn, attempt, and learn from your attempt. I highly recommend this for anyone interested in learning to read or write Arabic and develop speaking skills while gaining a great background into Arabic culture.
I would recommend but you must really be interested in learning Arabic; the teacher is amazing and so sweet!
Take this class! Ustaaza Aya and Ustaaz Alaa are absolutely incredible. You will learn so much from them. The class really focuses on talking and learning the basics of Arabic, and you'll find yourself speaking sentences in no time. The rest of the class really help define how you'll feel because a major part of class is interacting with peers!

Ustaaza Aya is so kind and she genuinely cares about each student and empowers us to become better Arabic speakers. You will learn so much from her– she is so sweet and friendly! I was very lucky to have her.
