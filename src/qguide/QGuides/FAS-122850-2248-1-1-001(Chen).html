If you think you will use GIS in your future career you MUST take this course! It is a fantastic balance of theoretical and technical learning that has equipped me to take data and transform it into visuals. The final project is perfect if you need to create maps for your thesis or capstone project, or have a particular topic that you are very interested in.
Maps can reveal hidden or unseen circumstances that might otherwise be overlooked, making them a valuable and multidisciplinary tool
If you're really passionate about learning about GIS, then I strongly recommend this course! Prof. Chen and Natalie were so excellent, and I really appreciated learning from them. At the same time, this course might be a lot and the assignments can be quite frustrating if GIS isn't really your thing. I had a passing interest, and found the course assignments to be a lot at times, but I am ultimately really grateful for what I did learn. The final project was especially satisfying and fulfilling, and I appreciate having something of my own at the end of the semester. Give it a try, maybe it'll spark something!
Wonderful class!
This might be my favorite course I've ever taken at Harvard; personally, it has inspired me to continue using GIS in my career in ecology going forward. GOV 1008 is a fantastic intro course for students who have little to no experience in GIS software. Students doing research (senior thesis, dissertation, etc.) are encouraged to work on final projects related to their research, so this class is great for people who have research questions in mind that can be answered with spatial analysis. Dr. Chen and TF Natalie are wonderful people who are committed to helping their students succeed. I highly recommend students take this course!!
A good class to help fulfill the data science program of study offered for government concentrators. Material was easy to pick up and assignments were manageable.
It's cool to know ArcGIS because it is applied to the manipulation and visualization of a lot of spatial data. The course moved a bit slow but the teaching staff were super nice.
ArcGIS Pro is a super powerful tool to learn how to use, and Dr. Chen is such an amazing teacher who is super passionate about teaching this to her students,
I loved this class! They made it so easy to learn the software and it was cool to get to apply it to whatever you want for the final project
TAKE THIS CLASS!! It is extremely friendly to first–time learners, so the pace is very manageable, and there are plenty of chances to ask clarifying questions in and out of class. The slow pace may feel boring for people hoping for something offering a more intense experience.
I would highly recommend taking this course. There are vast applications for the world of GIS that were previously unknown to me, and GIS is genuinely a fantastic skill to have (e.g. I used the skills gained in this class to make maps for another project for a different class). Dr. Chen's lectures are both interesting and engaging, and Natalie was one of the best TFs I've ever had in any course while at Harvard. Highly recommend!
